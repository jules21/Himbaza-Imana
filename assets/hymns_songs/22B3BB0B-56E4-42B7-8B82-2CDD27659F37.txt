303.Yesu, Mwungeri wanjye, Nd' agatam' undagire. Byose ndabitinyuka : Nta wuzakunyambura ! 

ii.Mwami, waramfiriye Kera ku Musaraba : Mbonye n'ibikomere Mu biganz' unteruje. 

iii.None,njye ngusingiza, Muri byose nkumvire ! Urukund' umfitiye, Ni rwo runyishimisha. 

iv.Yesu, Mwungeri mwiza, Non’ unyiyegereze !menyereza noneho, Nzinukwe kuzimira! 

v.Mwami Yes’ undohorere, nshe mu nzir’ifunganye; Nta cyo nanz' untegeke : Wow' uz’ ibinkwiriye! 

vi.Ngez' ah’ uri mw ijuru, Ibijya binyobera,Ni ho nzabimenyera, Nshim' uko wandagiye !
