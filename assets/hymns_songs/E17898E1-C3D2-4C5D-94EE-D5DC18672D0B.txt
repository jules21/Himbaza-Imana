140.Mwami, ndakwimitse : wime Mu mutima wanjye, Sinibagirwe ya mahwa : Njyan' i Gologota ! 

ref:Ibyabay’ i Getsemane N’ibyo wababajwe byose, Sinzabyibagirwe, Mwami: Njyan’ i Gologota. 

ii.Unyereke ya mva, Mwami,Aho bagushyize, Aho marayika wera Yaray' ataramye. 

iii.Nje nka Mariya, Mukiza, Kukurabukira; Nsanz’ imva y’ irimw ubusa; Nshimy’ Uwanzukiye! 

iv.Unkorer’ umusaraba,Niyange, mb’uwawe; Nsangire naw’ igikombe Wanyoye kubwanjye. Ndengera  Ndengera Mwami wanjye, ndengera
