106. Abantu bose batuye mw isi Ni bo bahawe Noheri. Ntidushobora kuzibagirwa Iyo Noheri twahawe. 

ref:Noheri nziza! Noheri nziza! Nimuze twese tuyiririmbe! Niy' itwibuts' agakiza kacu Noheri nziza ni Yesu. 

ii.N'igitangaza kukw abashumba Batanz' abandi Noheri. Nta cyubahiro bari bafite, Nyamar' Iman' ikor' ityo. 

iii.N'igitangaza kuk' uwo mwana Yavukiye mu nzu y'inka. Natw' abakene turashobora Kuririmbira Noheri. 

iv.Mur' iryo joro ritey' ukwaryo Ryaririmbwe na marayika Bat' Icyubahiro n'icy' Imana Nay' amahor' abe mw isi. 

v.Kand' uwo mwana nubwo yavutse Si bose bamwakiriye. Nyamara bose bamwakiriye Babay' abana b'Imana.
