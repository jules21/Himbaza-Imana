10.Nshim’umwami wo mw’ijuru Ntang’ibyo mushimisha Nari mboshyw’ arambohora Nari ndway’arankiza Tumushimire tumushimire Ni we mwam’uhoraho

ii.basogokuru kera bari mu  mubabaro abagirir’imbabazi tumuhimbaz’ibyo na none tumwizigire  kukw ar’uko yakoze

iii.ni data wa twese mwiza az’intege nke zacu nukw’aradukuyakuya ngw’aturinde satani duhimbaze imbabazi ze zitarangir’iteka

iv.yewe bamarayika beza nimushim’uwiteka namwe bera bo mw’ijuru bavuye mwisi yose mwese mushimane natwe nyirigir’uhoraho
