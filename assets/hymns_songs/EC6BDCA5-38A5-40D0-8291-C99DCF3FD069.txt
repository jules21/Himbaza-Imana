138.Yesu, Mukiza wanjye, Nguhany' ibyishimo Uyu mubiri wanjye, Ng’ub’uwawe rwose. 

ref:Akir' ituro ryanjye, Mwana w'UwIteka, Utum’ Umwuka Wera, Umanukiremo, 

ii.Sintinya na gatoya Kukwiyegurira, Kubw' imbabazi zawe, Murokozi wanjye.
