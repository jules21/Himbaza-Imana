368.Bene Data bizeye Yesu, Umucunguzi wacu mwiza, Mwitange mutagabanije, Nubwo benshi babinaniwe. 

Ref:Aheza mw ijuru  Tuzahurirayo, bagenzi.x2  

ii.Mwe gutinya ! Yes’ ubwe ni We Waneshej' urupfu n'ibyaha. Mwirinde, mwe guh' abanzi be Urwitwazo rwo kumutuka. 

iii.Yemw' ababyaw' ubwa kabiri, Yesu ni We mukunzi wacu; Ni We nzira y'abamukunda; Ni We mukuru w'ubugingo. 

iv.Yemw' abacunguwe na Yesu. Mugacunguzw’ amaraso ye. Yababohoye kwa Satani, Ababohoj' amaraso ye. 

v.Tur' ishyanga rye yironkeye, Yadukuye ku Musaraba.Az' adushoreye hw iminyago : None, tujye tumunezeza ! 

vi.Ntimugafatanye n'iby'isi; Mwemere kozwa n'amaraso: Mujye mwuzur' Umwuka Wera. Ibyo ni byo bimunezeza. 

vii.Yemw' abatura, ntimwihane, Mwe gushavuz' Umucunguzi ! Murabuz' abandi kwihana; Mukingiran’ Umwuka Wera ! 

viii.Yes' umugabe w'ubugingo, Ni we twese dutegereje. Ntituzongera gushoberwa Nk'abatahaw' Umwuka Wera.
