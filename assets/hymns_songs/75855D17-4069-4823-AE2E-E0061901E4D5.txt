188. Ndashaka gusa nawe,Yesu, Mucunguzi : Nta wigeze kukumva Uvug' urakaye. Ndashaka gusa nawe : Wasengaga kenshi, Wihereranye na So Kuri wa musozi.
	
ii.Ndashaka gusa nawe :Nubwo bakwangaga, Ntiwiturag' inabi Wagiriwe yose. Ndashaka gusa nawe : Wafashag' abantu; Ump' urukundo rwawe, Ruzabankundisha.
	
iii.Ndashaka gusa nawe :Wahamagaraga Abana bato bose, Maz' ukabigisha. Nyamara, sinsa nawe : Bose barabizi; Mukiza mwiz' umfashe, Nse nawe byukuri.
