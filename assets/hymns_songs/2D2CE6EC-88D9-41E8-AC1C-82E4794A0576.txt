70.Nimuze mwese kumv' inkuru nziza Z'agakiza kabwirizwa none. Yesu yabasanze :Arabashaka; Namwe muh' Umwami Imitima. 

ii.Mwitinyishwa n'uko mufit' ibyaha,Ngw azabang' abater' umugongo; Ntabwo yabigira :Yasezeranye Kwikir' uwihannye Umwizera. 

iii.Ni W' ubwe wizanye gukiz' abantu;Yavuye ku Witek' aza mw isi. Ngo tubon' ijuru,Yaradukunze; Yaratubambiwe;Yaravumwe ! 

iv.Abwira bos' ati : Jye nd' Ubugingo,Kand' unyizera wese ntazapfa. Nzamuha kubaho Mu bwami bwanjye; N'urupfu nzamuha Kurunesha.
