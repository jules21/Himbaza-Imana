430.Kera nar' umwangizi Mu mutima rwihishwa. Maze, mpura n'uw'Imana, Anyigish' ibya Yesu, 

ii.Uk' umuntu yakizwa Urubanza rw'ibyaha, Akabyarw' ubwa kabiri Kubw' urupfu rwa Yesu. 

iii.Ibyo maze kubyumva, Ntsindwa n'urubanz' ubwo, Meny’ ibyaha nagakoze Kuv' aho navukiye. 

iv.Nsaba Yes' imbabazi, Natur' ibyaha byanjye, Nizer' urukund' ankunda; Hanyum' arandokora. 

v.Kuv' ubwo, njya nzinukwa Iby'isi nakundaga, Menya yuko bizashira. Yes' ubw' azagaruka. 

vi.None, mfit' amahoro N'ubugingo bw'iteka. Yes' ampay' Umwuka Wera; Anesherez' iby'isi. 

vii.Ikimbabaza cyane, Kikanter' agahinda, N' ukureb' abari mw isi Banze kwitaba Yesu. 

viii.Abakijijwe twese Tubabarir' ab'isi,Tubabwire n'Ubutumwa : Ni wo murimo wacu.
