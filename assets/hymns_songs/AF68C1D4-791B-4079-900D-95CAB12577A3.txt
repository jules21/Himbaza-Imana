100. Nzaririmb' igitangaza Yes' Umwami yakoze; Ko yavuy' i We mw ijuru Ngw amfir’ i Gologota.  

ref:Nzaririmb' igitangaza Yes' Umwami yakoze: Ndi kumwe n'abo mw ijuru Imbere ya ya ntebe. 

ii.Nari nzimiye nk' intama Maze Yes' arambona; Nukw anshyira ku bitugu, Ansubiza mu nzira. 

iii.Nar' impumyi, mfit' ubwoba Nshits' intege, mbabaye, Njanjaguritse, nihebye, Maze Yes' arankiza. 

iv.Ndacyabon' ibimbabaza; Haracyar' ibindushya; Nyamar' ubwo turi kumwe, Nzi yuko nta cyo nzaba. 

v.Azanyuza no mu rupfu,Nambuke na rwa Ruzi; Maze, ni mfata hakurya, Nzasangay' abo nkunda
