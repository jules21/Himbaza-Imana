127 a.Mukiza, numvis’ ijwi Ryawe ry'imbabazi Rimpamagara ngo nozwe N'amaraso yawe. 

ref:Nditabye, Yesu, Ndaje, Mukiza. Amaraso wavuye Anyoz' antunganye. 

ii.Dor' ukonje, nihebye, Ibyaha ni byinshi: Byose ndabikuzaniye, Naw’ubikureho. 

iii.Kand' uramp’umutima Wuzuy' urukundo, Wuzuye kwizera na ko N'amahoro menshi. 

iv.Kand’uzajy'umfashisha Imbabazi nyinshi. Ibyo wansezeranije Uzabisohoza. 

v.Nshim’amaraso yawe, Ankurahw ibyaha; Mpimbaz' imbaraga zawe Zinkiz' intege nke.

b.Numvis’ijwi ryawe, Yesu, ndagusanze, Ndaje, Mucunguz' unyoze Mu maraso yawe. 

ref:Mwami w'abami  waramfiriye.Nje gupfa hamwe nawe, Ye, ku Musaraba. 

ii.Numvis' ijwi ryawe, Riti: Nyizigira ! Unkomereze kwizera, Kandi, tugumane. 

iii.Numvis' ijwi ryawe Ryinjiiye muri jye, Rimbwiriza kujya nkunda Nkuko naw' unkunda. 

iv.Numvis' Ijwi ryawe,Wowe, wamfiriye! Mwami, mbane naw' iteka; Ni Wow' unyizeza.
