139.Mukiza Yesu, ntabara, Kukw abantu babiri Bari mu mutima wanjye, Umwe akunda ugoma. 

ref:Ndengera  Ndengera Mwami wanjye, ndengera 

ii.Umwe ni we Mwuka wawe Unyiringiz’ijuru; Und’ahor anyoshya nabi, Ashaka kunshumuza. 

iii.Mwami, sinkor' ibyo nkunda, Nubwo mpora mbyifuza.  Ibibi byo ndabikora, Nubw’iteka njya mbyanga. 

iv.Murokoz' umucyo wawe Umurikire neza, Ngo wa muntu mubi nanga Atamperana rwose. 

v.Iyo nirwanya ntyo, Mwami, Nta mahoro mba mfite. Ndagukunda, ndagushaka  Mfasha,nesh' uwo mubi 

vi.Kiz' imbata yaw' irushywa, Ihor'ikuganyira; Umpe n'ububasha bwawe Ngo njye nsinda Satani. 

vii.Ndashak' uburuhukiro Mu bwami bwawe bwiza; Mfasha ngo wa wundi nanga Atambuza kujyayo.
