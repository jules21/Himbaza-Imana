364. Yes' Umwami ndamubonye, Kandi sinkimwimura; Yemeye kunyoz' i byaha Byambuzag' ubugingo.

Ref:Nta kirut' Umwami Yesu Mw’ isi cyangwa mw ijuru. Ni We watuguz' amaraso Kera ku Musaraba.

ii. Amaras' arantangaza : Yarampembuye mpfuye. Ampesh' amahoro menshi No kubaho kw'iteka. 

iii.Ubwo wampinduy' umwana, Non' umpindur' ingeso, Kugira ng’ undeba wese Aguhimbaze, Yesu. 

iv.Ndagushima ko wanshatse Kandi ko wantaruye, Ukanyogesh' amaraso Kubw’ imbabazi zawe. 

v.Mwami Yes' ump' urukundo, Rwo wakunz' abo mw isi. Umpe kugukunda rwose, Nkunde n'abawe bose. 

vi.Warampfiriye, Mukiza : Noneho, mpfane nawe ! Kand' usohoze muri jye ibyo kugushimisha.
