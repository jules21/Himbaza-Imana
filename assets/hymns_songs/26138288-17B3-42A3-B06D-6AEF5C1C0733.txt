ref: Uh' Umwami Yes' ahw aba : Umvir' Ijwi rye ryinginga  Kingur' umutima wawe, Yinjiremo, yimemo  

ii.Dor' imirimo n'iby'isi Bikwihariy' umutima : Ntagufitemw ahw atura,Nubwo yagupfiriye.
