432.Abo mur'iyi si—Izashira Nta cyo mwibajije—Ku by'imperuka ? 

Ref:Ntimwitegereje—Icyiza twabonye ? None, mwisigara—Mu butamenya ! 

ii.Dor' icyo twahawe—Cy'igiciro : Twahaw' ubugingo—Butazashira. 

iii.Yesu ni We zuba—Ritwakira; Rituma tureba :—Tumusingize ! 

iv.Isi yo mw ijuru—N' ihebuza. Turek' iby'iyi si,—Tuhagabane ! 

v.Ushak' ubugingo—Apfirw' ibye, Nk'ushak' izahabu,—Ukw apfirw'ibye. 

vi.Yemw' abameze nka —Muka loti, Mwikund' iby'iyi si—Izarimbuka ! 

vii.Twebw' abakijijwe,—Dutambuke; Ntitugakebuke,—Ngo duhomberwe !
