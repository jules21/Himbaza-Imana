354.Man' ishobora byose, Umv' uko nkwinginga : Umpishurire Yesu, Meny' ibyo yankoreye. 

Ref:Yarahemuwe Ku Musaraba: Mpumur' amaso, ndebe Kw ari jye yazize, 

ii.Mbony' inkon' akubiswe N'amahwa yambaye; Mbonye na za mbereri N'umutim' ucumiswe. 

iii.Yemeye kwiranguza Igikombe kibi : Yakinyoye kubwanjye, Maz' amp' ubugingo bwe. 

iv.Reb' uyu Mucunguzi Urupfu yapfuye  Nta n'ikibi yakoze: Nuko sinzarimbuka ! 

v.Ni jyewe yabambiwe,Uwamugomeye. Yesu, ndagushimiye : Non' uture muri jye. 

vi.Yesu, we kunkundira Ko ngusigirira. Ku Musaraba wawe, Ni ho nkuy' ubugingo. 

vii.Mana, ndagushimira K' utimanye Yesu, Ngw amfire nkiraniwe. Yewe, warankunze pe !
