129.Ndeger’Umusaraba, naniwe, nkennye, mpumye; Nta kindi kindi nshaka : Ngusabye kunkiz’ubu. 

ref:Mwami ndakwiringiye Ayi Mucunguzi wanjye; Dor’uko nkunamiye  Mwami Yes’unkiz  ubu. 

ii.Nakwifujije kera,Ndi mu ngoyi z'ibyaha; Maz’umbwiranez' uti: Ngukiz’ ibicumuro ! 

iii.Ubu ndakwihereje: End’ibyo mfite byose, Ubwenge n'imbaraga; Byose Yes’ubyakire. 

iv.Nizey’ibyo wavuze N'amaraso wavuye; Ndicuza gutinda ntyo: None, Yesu, nyakira ! 

v.Yesu, non’ungezemo ; Urakaza, Mukiza ! Nta n'ineng' umbaraho  Shimwa, shimwa, Nyakira !
