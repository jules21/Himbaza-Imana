352.Muze kureb' i Gologota Yes' ukw ashinyagurirwa, Murebe Yes' ukundwa na Se Amenets' umutima we. 

ref:Nitw' apfira ku Musaraba  Tumwizere twihannye. Bantu b'Umwami. mushime Yes' Umucunguzi wanyu  

ii.Bamwambits' ikamba ry'amahwa: N' ibibi byacu yazize. Ubuntu n'urukundo byawe, Mbe, twabirondora dute ? 

iii.Ni mwumv' ijwi ry' uko yinginga :Ngo, Dat' ubababarire ! Natwe twes' adusabir' atyo, Tw' abanyabyaha yazize.
	
iv.Nimwumve mwes' uko gutaka, Uko yatatse cyan' ati : Mbe, Mana yanjye, Mana yanjye, N'iki kikundekesheje ? 

v.Nimwongere mwumv' iryo jwi rye,Ngo, Byose birarangiye ! Umwuka we wenda guhera Ati : Dat' uz' unyakire ! 

vi.Dor' uko tugupfukamiye.Turakuramya, Mukiza. Kand' ibyaremwe byose na byo Bizagusingiza, Yesu. 

vii.Dufatany' ubugingo bumwe,Dushim' Uwadupfiriye. No mu bugingo no mu rupfu, Yesu Mwam' umbwire byose!
