392.Basirikare ba Kristo, mubyuke, Murasane n'isi n'umubiri n'Umubi; Muze murwan' iyo ntambara nziza :Dor' ibendera mwahawe n' Umusaraba. Nimumuyoboke ! Arabarengera ! Tumufite, tuzanesh' Umwanzi Satani.

ii.Ntumwa za Yesu, ngo, Mwese mugende,Mujye mu bihugu byose byo mur'iyi si. Mwumvish' abaremwe bos' ubutumwa, Muti : Yesu yabacunguj' amaraso ye. Muti : Mumwemere, nukw ababature! Ni We wadutumye kubwa rwa rukundo rwe.

iii.Yemwe Bakristo, mubyuke n'ingoga! Nimucan' amatabaza, mube mas' ubu.Wa Mukw' araje : mumusanganire !  Azajyan' abiteguy' i bwami mw ijuru. Mwitange, noneho  Ntimugapfapfane  Dor' araje niko yadusezeranije !
