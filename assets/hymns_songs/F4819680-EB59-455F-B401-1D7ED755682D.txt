108. Yew' usonzey' agakiza, Ng' ubabarirw' ibyaha. Umv' agakuru k'imvaho Yuko Yes' agushaka.   

ref:Uhamagawe na Yesu, Na Yesu Mukiza. Aragushak' uyu munsi Mwemerer' agukize. 

ii.Nawe wabay' inzimizi Kuko wahunze Yesu, Menya ko yagupfiriye. Ngwin' umusange none. 

iii.Naw' uhamagawe none Wite kur' iryo jwi rye. Reka gutind' uyu munsi. N'uwawe w'agakiza. 

iv.Harihw abumvis' iri jwi Bararisuzugura, hanyuma bajy' ikuzimu Kimwe na wa mutunzi.
