136.a. Ubugingo bwanjye bwose,Yesu, ndabukwihereye; Kand' iminsi yanjye yose,Njye ngushimisha ntasiba. 

ii.Amaboko yanjye, Mwami, Ajy'akor' iby' untegeka; Ibirenge byanjye na byo Bijye bijy' ah' unkundiye.  

iii.Ijwi ryanjye na ryo n'uko Rijye rikuririmbira; Kand' akanwa kanjy’ iteka Kavug' ibyo wankoreye. 

iv.Ibyo mfite byose na byo. Simbikwima, Mwami Yesu; Kand' ubwenge bwanjye na bwo Jy'ubukoresh iby' ushaka. 

v.Hindur' umutima wanjye, Nkund’iby' ukunda byonyine. Sinzigeng' ukund' ahubwo Ndakugandukiye, Mwami.  

vi.Urukundo rwanjye rwose Ndugukunde gusa, Yesu; Nkomeza, mb’uwaw’iteka: Ndakwihaye, sinkitwara ! 
                                                                                                                                                                             
b.numvise ijwi ry'Umukiza, Anyihamagarira; None, mutim' ungomesha, Umvir' Umucunguzi. 

ii.Akir' ubugingo bwanjye: Mwam' ubwitegekere; Ubwimemo, budakurwa Mu mategeko yawe. 

iii.Ujy'ukoresh' uk' ushaka Ubu bugingo bwanjye; byanjye ndabigutuye: Databuja, nd' uwawe. 

iv.No mu bihe byanjye byose, Komeza kunshorera N'imbaraga zawe zose; Ujy'untegek' iteka.
