220. Ubwo nzabon’ Umukiza Ntumbira mu maso he, Nzanyurwa mwitegereje, Ari We wamfiriye. 

ref:Nzamubona, Yes' Umwami, Turikumwe mu ijuru : Nzamwirebera mu maso, Njye musingiz' iteka! 

ii.Nkiri mu isi mu mubiri, Mbur' uko mwirebera. Urya muns' urenda kuza, Mbon' ubwiza bwe bwinshi ! 

iii.Amakuba azaba ashize Nta byaha, nta mwijima : Ibigande biganduwe, Tuzishim' imbere ye ! 

iv.Ni mwirebera mu maso, Nzicwa n'umunezero. Nzamumeny' Uwo wankunze, Akaz' akanshungura
