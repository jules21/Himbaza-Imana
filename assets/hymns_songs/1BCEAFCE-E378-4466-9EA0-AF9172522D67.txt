61. Kubw' urukundo rwinshi rwatumy' aza kunshaka, Anshyira ku bitugu anjyana mu rugo rwe.  Haririmbw' indirimbo nziza Ziririmbwa na marayika.

ref:Yaj' aje kunshaka kugira ngw ankize. Yamvanye mu musayo w'urupfu. Yanshyize mu rugo rwe rw'intama.

ii.Yesu, Mwungeri mwiza, Yanzuye mu mutima. Aherakw ambwir' ati, Mwana Wanjye nakunze.  Uko ni kw iryo jwi rye ryiza Ryahojej' umutima wanjye.

iii.Ntabwo nakwibagirwa ko yavuy' amaraso. Ubwo bamwambikaga na rya kamba ry'amahwa, Kandi ku musaraba  niho Nshimir' Umwami Yesu Kristo.

iv.Ubu nsigaye ngendera mu mucyo w'ukuri. Mfit’ amasezerano mur' iyo nzira ncamo, Nzahora nshim' Umwami  Yesu Iteka ryose nezerewe.

v. Ibihe birihuta ndindiriy'igitondo, Ubw' uzampamagara unyinjiza mw ijuru. Nzahagarar' imbere yawe Nezerewe kandi ndirimba.
