246.I Gologota, ku Giti, Ni ho Yesu yabambwe; Yabambiwe kugira Ngo tubone kudapfa. 

ii.Yes’ amaze kunesha, Avug’ ijwi rirenga, Abivuga yeruye,Ati: Birarangiye ! 

iii.Amaze kuvug’ atyo, Aherakw arunama, Yunamishwa n’ibyaha Byanjye nanze kwihana.  

iv.none ansabye imbabazi,nihane,mukureyo,mumanure ku giti,aruhuke umutima. 

v.abantu batihana bongeye kumubamba,n'umuruho munini,umubabaza iteka. 

vi.twari twararuhijwe,twiganyira,twihebye,none turaruhutse:Yesu ni uburuhukiro 

vii.nuko abishwe n'ibyaha,ni muhunge,mukire muze,mubikurwemo:Yesu ni uburuhukiro 

viii.Mukiza,ndakwihaye ni wowe wantaruye,uko nzaba ndi kose,Yesu,nzagusingiza.
