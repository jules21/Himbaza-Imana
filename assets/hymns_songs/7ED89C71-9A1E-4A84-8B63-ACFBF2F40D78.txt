248.Hafi y'Umusaraba,aho ntura iteka,niho mperwa ku buntu amazi y'ubugingo. 

ref:nirata umusaraba kugeza ubwo nzaba mbonye uburuhukiro hakurya y'uruzi

ii.nahageze nshobewe,nsinzwe n'urubanza,Yesu arambabarira,angotesha urukundo 

iii.mwami Yesu unyibutse uwo musaraba,kugira ngo ngendere mu gicucu cyawo. 

iv.Ni w’utuma nemera Kwang' iby'isi rwose, Kukw ariw’unkundisha Yesu wamfiriye. 

v.Hafi y'Umusaraba, Ni ho ntur’iteka, Ntegerej’ igihe cyo Kwambuka rwa Ruzi.
