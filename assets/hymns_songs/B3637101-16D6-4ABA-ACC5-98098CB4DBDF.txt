98. Abazaba bakijijwe bazateranira hamwe Ku meza mw ijuru hamwe na Yesu Mu bwami budashira,
 bazareb' uburanga bwe. Bameny' uko yabakunze.Bazahora baririmbira mw ijur' iteka.

ref:Bazava mu nyanja zose, Bazava mu byago byose, Bave mu misozi, bave mu mataba Bager' i mbere y 'Imana.
 Bazambar' imyenda yera, bareb' Umukiza ubwo. Niwe wabapfiriye Kera ku musaraba.
	
ii.Bazateran' ari benshi abakirijw' ino mw isi. Ibyago n'urupfu, cyang' umwijima Ntibizabah' ukundi.  Nta bya kera bizabaho Hazaba harihw ibishya. Hazabah' umunezer' utagir' amakemwa.
	
iii.Niwitegerez' urugi, ni wowe rukinguriwe, Na Yesu Mukiz' arakubwir' ati: Ngwino nawe winjire bagenzi bacu bariyo
 Baradutegereje pe! Ba maraika bishimiye kuzatubona.
