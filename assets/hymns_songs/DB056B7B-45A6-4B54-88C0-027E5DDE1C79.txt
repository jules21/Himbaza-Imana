293.Uko njya mbona mw 'ijuru Njya mbwir' Umwami, nti Ndakwinging' unyigishe Kuguh' umutima. 

ii.Iteka mu gitondo,Njya mbwir' Umwami, nti : Nyiriz' imbere yawe, Mana yanjye nkunda. 

iii.Kenshi cyane mu munsi, Njya mbwir' Umwami, nti : Ni Wowe turi kumwe: Uhor' ushimisha. 

iv.Kand' iy' ijoro rije, Njya mbwir' Umwami, nti : Mukiz' ureba hose, Mpa ku byiza byawe ! 

v.Kugez' igihe nzapfa,Nzabwir’ Umwami, nti: Ni kuri Wowe, Data, Njya mperw' umugisha.
