94. Nezerwa, mutima wanjye, Kuko warons' agakiza. Namaze kubabarirwa Nd' uwa Yesu nawe n'uwanjye.  

ii.Yankijije mu mutima N'umuganga mwiza cyane. Kandi mu Mwuka we Wera Nihw abatiriz' abantu be.  

iii.Nzi ko nanditswe mw ijuru Kubw’ Umwami wanjye Yesu. Nd' ubutunzi bw'Umukiza, Nzahora nd' uw' iteka ryose.   

iv.None mu mutima wanjye Ndaririmb' Imana Data. Buri munsi ni we nshuti Yo kunezeza muri byose.  

v.Yankuyehw ibyaha byose Nanjye sinzabisubira. Kandi Yes' aracyakiza Abamusanga buri gihe. 

vi.Indirimbo zimushima Zikwiriye kuba nyinshi. Mu mutima wanjye naho Hati: hashimw' Iman'iteka!
