45.Ndagushaka, Mwami Yesu Ntabwo nakwishoboza. Ndifuza k’ umpora hafi, Ukajy' ump' imbaraga.

ref:Nta cyo nti... nya noneho.., Nkore byo... s' untegeka... Njye nguku.. .rikira hose, Nkumvira, nguhakwaho.

ii.Ndagushaka, Mwami Yesu Jye nd' umunyantege nke. Uzamber' umujyanama. Nta wundi mbitezeho.

iii.Ndagushaka, Mwami Yesu,Aho ndi mur'iyi si, Mu muvumbi no ku zuba, Mu bimbabaza byose.

v.Ndagushaka, Mwami Yesu, Hos' uzajy’ unyobora; Ngeze kuri wa Mugezi, Uzamfashe nywambuke.
