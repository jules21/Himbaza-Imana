319.Aho mwumvis' ijwi ry'Umwami ? Ati, Basore, mubyuke ! Turi hano, tugandukire Rwos’ amategeko yawe. 

Ref:Tuguhay ubusore bwacu N’urukundo rwacu, Mwami: Mu ntege nke zacu twuzure Tujye duhoran' iteka. 

ii.Twifuza cyan' umucyo wawe N'urukundo n'amahoro; Twishiman' ibendera yawe, Tuyuyane mw isi yose. 

iii.Dufatish' ukuboko kwawe, Ujy' udushorer' iteka. Nubwo duterwa n'ibitwoshya, Ujy' ubidukiza byose. 

iv.Duh' umwete nk' Intumwa zawe, Duhore dukora nka zo; Duhwane na zo mu kwizera No kugupfir' ubishatse.
