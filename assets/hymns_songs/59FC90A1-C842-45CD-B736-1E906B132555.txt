425. Kera narabatijwe Kandi  ndakomezwa, Ntari nabon' amaraso Y'Umwana w'Imana. 

Ref:Ko wampfiriye, Ndakwitur' iki?Ndaguh' uyu mutima N'ubugingo bwanjye ! 

ii. Mwa bafit' ibyaha mwe, Nimuze, mukizwe : Yesu ni We bukiriro Bw'abantu banduye. 

iii. Mwumv’ aho dukirizwa : Ni ku Musaraba. Utahagez' ashobewe Ntabw' ababarirwa. 

iv.Mwami Yes' unyemere, Njy' ah' ur' uko nd' uku, Unyuhagiz' amaraso, Ayo wamviriye. 

v.Yesu, warampfiriye,Uwakugomeye; Nkuruwe n'uko wankunze; Ngusanze non' ubu. 

vi.Noneho, ndakwinginze, Nshyira mu maraso, Ay'igiciro wavuye ; Bizambuza kugwa. 

vii.Kand' iyo ngiye kugwa, Arankuyakuya; Maz' akanyigiza hafi Kukw ankunda cyane. 

viii.N'ubu ndacyabanguka Kuyob’ inzira ye; Nakwihana, nkamwisunga, Angaruramw ubwo. 

ix.Uwiteka Rurema Na Yesu yabyaye N'Umwuka Wera bahwanye, Nsingiz' ubutatu.
