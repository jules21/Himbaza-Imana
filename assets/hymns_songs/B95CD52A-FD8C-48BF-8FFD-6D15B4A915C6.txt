6.Mu gihe cya Noheri, Turebye mu muvure. Dusanga Yes' aryamye mu buryo bwa gikene. 

ii.Mukiza wanjye mwiza, wazanywe n'urukundo. Wambabariy' ibyaha. wangize kub' uwawe. 

iii.Yesu ni wowe nshaka, ur' inshuti y'abana. Mukiza sinzongera gutegekwa n'ibyaha. 

iv.Nzahora nshima Yesu mu bihe byanjye byose. Yazanywe mur' iyi si, kuducungura twese.
