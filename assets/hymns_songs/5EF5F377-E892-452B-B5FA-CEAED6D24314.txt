325.Ababatijwe none,Ubarinde, Mwami, Ub' umubyeyi wabo,Kukw ar' abangizi. Bajya mw ijuru bate ? Ntabwo bakwijyana, Keretse bakwizeye, Wowe, Mucunguzi.

ii.Bashakish' urukundo,Ujy' ubaramira. Bakwiringire, Mwami,Bahunge Satani; Muvunyi Yesu, ngwino, Ubakiz' ibibi. Twese tuguhimbaze, Ng' udukiz' iteka.

iii. Mwam' ubwawe wenyine, Ubabatirishe Umwuka wawe wera,Bajye bakwizera; Twese tuzafatanye, Tuzab' umwe rwose, Duhore dukundana Nkuko wadukunze.
