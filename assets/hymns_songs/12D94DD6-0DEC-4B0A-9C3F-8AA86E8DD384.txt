51.Muze musange Yes' ubakunda, Mwese nimuze, mwe kuzuyaza. Yes’ arashaka yuko mukira,Nuko, mumwitabe.

ref:Yes' ubw’ uzaza, tuzishima twese, Tujye kuban’ akaramata nawe, dutandukanye n'isi n'ibyaha Tubanye mw ijuru.

ii.Mwese nimuze, mwitabe Yesu, Abaruhur’ abah' amahoro. Nuko, mumuh' imitima yanyu, Yos’ ayibarire.

iii. Wibuke yukw’igihe gikuze : Nta n'umw' umenya kw atinda mw isi, Umwami Yes' ageze bugufi; Tebuk'umwakire!
