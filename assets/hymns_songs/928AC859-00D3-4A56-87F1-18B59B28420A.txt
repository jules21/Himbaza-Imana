69.Igihugu cyiza kiradutegereje mw ijuru, Kizicarwamo n'abatowe na Yesu. Kand' iminsi turimo nayo nikw ihita ningoga Uwihangany' azaragw' icyo gihugu.

ref:Dor' ubwami bw'Imana buregereje! Dor' ubwami bw'Imana buregereje! Mutima wanjye ba maso, Utungan’iminsi  yose. Dor' ubwami bw'Imana buregereje!

ii.Twizeye rwose kuzabon' icyo gihug' uko kiri, Dutegereje gus' uwaducunguye. Ibimenyetso bitwereka
 kw agiye kugaruka, Bitwerekako wa munsi wegereje.

iii.Kand' ibyanditswe na byo bivuga , ko Yes' aza vuba. Kand' ubw' azagaragarira mu bicu. Haleluya, haleluya  azaboha wa mugome, Umwe wagerageje kuturimbura.

iv.Kand' abantu benshi barananiwe kutegereza, ubakanguze vuba bya bimenyetso. Kukw ijambo ry'Imana  rishaka gusohora vuba. N'ukuri tubona ko Yes' aza vuba.
