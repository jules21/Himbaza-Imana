8.Umucyo wabonekeye bose bari mu mwijima. Ubw' abungeri bumvaga indirimb' iva mw ijuru.

ref:Icyubahiro mw ijuru, Kib' icy' Iman' Ihoraho. Nahw amahor' abe mw isi. Tuyishime duhimbaza.

ii.Mu murwa wa Betlehemu, none havuts' uruhinja. N'umunezer' i Yudaya, kukw Iman' ibacunguye.

iii.Mariya yambits' Umwana utwambaro tw'uruhinja. Kandi mu muvure w'inka ni ho yamuryamishije.
