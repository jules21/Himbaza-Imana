83.Umutwaro wanjye, Krisito, Warawikoreye. Bakubambye ku Musaraba,Ni jyewe wazize; Unkuraho wa mutwaro : Sinkiwikoreye ! 

ii.Igikombe cyanjye cyarimo Kuvumwa n'urupfu; Nuk' ucyiranguza kubwanjye; Ibyo bisharira, Uherak’ ubisimbuza Ibyiza by'ijuru  

iii.Uwiteka wang' ibyaha pe Yabanguy' inkoni Ariko si jye yakubise, Ahubwo ni Wowe ,iyo mibyimba Krisito, Ni yo yankijije ! 

iv.Haguy’ iy' inkundura, Yesu,Jye nkunamamw ubwo; Iguhitiraho ku Giti, Jye ntiyanyagira. Yarakunshocaguriye; None, hareyutse. 

v.so yakuy inkota ye yera,Aragusogota;  Umutima waw' uhinduka Urwubati rwayo. Non' iyo nkot' iragimbye : Nta cy' ikintwar' ubu ! 

vi.Jye napfanye nawe, Krisito, Ubwo wamfiraga; Urazuka, nzukana nawe,Uraz' unturamo. Non' untungany' unyeze de, Uzangez' i Wawe.
