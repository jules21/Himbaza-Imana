85.Urukundo rw'Umukiza Ni nk' amazi menshi cyane. Ameze nk'isoko nziza Idudubiza muri we. 

Yesu ni we wuguruye Rwa rurembo rwo mw ijuru, Kugira ngo ndwinjiremo Kubw' ubuntu bwinsh' agira. 

ii.Nababay' iminsi myinshi, Nasaga n'inyon' ihigwa. Natakiy' Umwami Yesu Nawe ntiyanyirukanye. 

iii.Mwiyumvir' igitangaza, Yanyogej' ibyaha byose, Kuber' ubwo bunt' agira Ndirimban' umunezero. 

iv.Mu gitondo cy'agakiza Nzagera mur' iryo rembo. Kuber' urukund' afite Nzinjira mur' uwo murwa.
