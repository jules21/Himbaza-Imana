423.Reka nshim’ Umwami Yesu Kuko yankunze rwose : Yarampinduye mb' umwana, Ngo tubane mw ijuru. 

Ref:Reka nshim' Umwami Yesu Ko yamvanye mu byaha; Noneho, mfit' amahoro Arut' ay'isi cyane. 

ii.Mur'iyi si dutuyemo Yuzuy' uburiganya, Ntabwo yabasha guhaza Iby' imitim' ishaka.  

iii.Kand' Umwuka wawe wera,Abamuhawe bose, Ni W' ujy' abazinukisha Kwifuz' i by'isi byose, 

iv.Ujye wirukan’ ubwenge Bwa Satan' anzanira Bwo kuntandukanya nawe, Isoko y'ubugingo. 

v.Yemw' ababyawe n'Imana,Mujye muhirimbana, Mugere ku Musaraba, Mubone kunesh' isi. 

vi.Yes' amaz’ imyaka myinshi Ajy'atwihanganira; Nimuze, dusig' ab'isi, Babone kutwifuza. 

vii.Mwe kujya muvuga Yesu,Uwo mwumvany' abandi; Mujye mumureb’ ubwanyu, Ari ku Musaraba. 

viii.Mwe kujya mukebaguza No gusubir' inyuma Mu byo mwahozemo kera,Mudahemura Yesu !
