277.Mwam' ubugingo bwacu ni nk'umunsi: Ni buke rwose ntiburama cyane; Kand' ibyishimo byabwo  birashira:Mwam' udahinduka, tugumane.

ii.Gumana nanjye, kuk' umuns' ukuze, Dore burije, Mwami, ndagushaka; Ni mbur' abandi bose bo kumfasha, Tugumane,  Mwami, tugumane

iii.Iminsi yose mba ngushaka, Mwami, Iyo Satan' antey' andwanya cyane, Nta wundi mfit' ubasha kuntabara; Tugumane, Mwami, tugumane.

iv.Singir’ umwanzi n'umwe turi kumwe, Kand' amakuba ntambabaza cyane; Intorezo y'urupfu yaracitse; Byose ni byiza, ni tugumana.

v.Amaso n' aremb' uzambonekere, Nzabon' ubwiza bwawe bwo mw ijuru, Nzasiga vub' umwijima wo mw isi, No mw ipfa ryanjye, tuzagumane !
