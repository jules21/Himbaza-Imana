119.Yesu murutisha byose:Nta kindi nkifuza. Iminsi yos' aramfasha,Ngo nkomere, ne kugwa. Iyo ndushye njya kuri We;
 Nta wund' unduhura nkawe We. Yanyujuje ... ibyishimo .. ! N' uwanjye !

ii.Yesu murutisha byose :Amfasha naniwe. Itek’ amp' imigisha Mbur' uko nabivuga. Atang' izuba n'imvura  Ngo nez' imyak' inkwiriye Angabira ...  byose nkena .. ! N' uwanjye ! 

iii.Yesu murutisha byose : Nzajya mukorera Kukw ar' uwo kwiringirwa; Sinzamuhemukira. Nkurikira Yesu Turi kumwe,
 nta cyo  nzaba. Arankunda .. ! Arandinda . N' uwanjye !

Iv. Yesu murutisha byose  N' inshuti yanjye pe Nta wundi nabona nka We;Nzahora mwizigira Yampay' impan' ihebuje 
 Ni yo bugingo bw'iteka. Aranyuze .. ! Musingize N' uwanjye !
