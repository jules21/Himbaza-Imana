179.Iy' urushye, iy'uremerewe, Bibwire Yesu, bibwire Yesu ! Iy' ukumbuy' abakunzi bawe, Byos' ubimuganyire ! 

ref:Bibwire Yesu, bibwire Yesu ! Ni We nshut' ihebuje, Nta n'indi wabona imeze nka We; Byos' ubimuganyire  

ii.Ibyo byago, ndetse n'amarira, Bibwire Yesu, bibwire Yesu ! Bya byaha bihishwe bikunesha,Byos' ubimuganyire ! 

iii.Ubw' utiny' indwara no gupfusha. Bibwire Yesu, bibwire Yesu  Ubwo wibaz' iby'umunsi w'ejo Byos' ubimuganyire! 

iv.Mbes' ugir' ubwoba bwo kuzapfa ? Bibwire Yesu, bibwire Yesu  Ntuzi yuk' Umwami Yes' azaza ? Byos' ubimuganyire!
