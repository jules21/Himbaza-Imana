3.Mwa bari mw isi yose mwe, Nimuhimbaz' Uwiteka. Muhore mumukorera, Kukw ibyo bidukwiriye.

ii.Mumenye yuk' Uwiteka Ari we watwiremeye; Turi n'intama aragira, Tur' izo mu mukumbi we.

iii.Mwinjire mu marembo ye, Mugere no mu rugo rwe, Muhore mu musingiza, Muhimbaze n'izina rye.

iv.Umwam' Imana ni mwiza; Imbabazi n'ubuntu bye Iteka bizaboraho; Mwese mushim’ izina rye.
