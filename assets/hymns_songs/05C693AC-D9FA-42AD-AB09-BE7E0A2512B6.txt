69.Nkuko Mose yamanits' inzoka ku giti,Abarembejwe bakayireba, Bagakir' uburibwe, bakaba bazima Kubw’ imbabazi z'Uwiteka,  Ni ko na Yesu Yatumanikiwe ku Musaraba kera : Tumurebe, tumwiringire.

ii.Mutim' ushavuye, reb' Uwagupfiriye;Reba Yesu wakumanikiwe ! , Munyabyah' umutumbir' aragukiz' ubu,Umureb' urakira rwose ! Reba, wizere ! Kubabarirwa kwawe kuv’ i Gologota :' Reba, reba ku Musaraba 

iii. N'iki cyatumy' Umwana w' Iman'ababazwa ?umv' icyatumy' abambwa ku giti, Kand' icyatumye yambikw' ikamba ry' amahwa: Ni ku bwawe n'ibyaha byawe ! Reba, wizere ! Dor' urupfu n' urwe, nahw imyandu n' iyawe : Reba, reba ku Musaraba.

iv.Naho warir' ukicuz' ukanababara,Ntibyarih' ibicumuro byawe. Dor' impongan' ikwiriy' ibyaha wakoze N' amaraso ya Yesu masa.Reba, wizere ! Wizer' Uwakubambiw' arakurokora : Reba, reba ku Musaraba,

v.Yagukirishije ya mibyimba yagize;Ni na We gukiranuka kwawe. Yakubeshejeho, war' uwo kurimbuka : Wakir' ubwo bugingw aguha.Reba,wizere ! Yes’ ubw' atagipfa, tuzabanaho na we : Reba, reba ku Musaraba.
