50.Muze mwenyine, twihererane, Munsang’ ahantu hatar' abantu; Dor’ imiruhw irabarembeje; Urusaku rurabaruhije.

ii.Murek’ ibyifuzwa n'ab'iyi si, Mushak' ibyahishw’ abanyabwenge; Jyewe na Data tubana namwe : Ntimuri mwenyine, turi kumwe.

iii. Murajye mumbwir’ ibyo mwibwira, Ibyo muvuga n'ibyo mukora, Mu miruho yanyu munaniwe; Bimenywa n'uz' imigambi yanyu.

iv.Inzir' iraruhije  mwitange, Mutazananirwa mukigenda; Mury' umutsim' uter' imbaraga, Munywe no ku mazi y'ubugingo.

v.Nuko, mukor' imirimo yanyu; Nzajya mbafasha mu mibabaro; Uwitanga nzamugororera; Ndaza vub' umusek utambitse.
