249.Nitegerej’Umusaraba Wo Yesu yadupfiriyeho, Nywubonye, ndamutakambira Ngw ambabarire. 

ref:Yes’umwami ahimbazwe izina rye ryogezwe  Yanyogesheje amoroso ye: Yes’ahimbazwe. 

ii.Iman’ ishimwe kukw inkiza Imbaraga z'ibyaha zose. Bituma Yes’ambamo neza, Akantunganya. 

iii.Ya soko nziza yoz' ibyaha, Nejejwe n'uko nayinjiyemo; Ni yo Yes’ahor’ankirisha; Ni y’ anyejesha. 

iv.Ngwino kw isoko ngari nziza ! Ca bugufi, wa mutindi we! Jya ku birenge by'Umukiza,Ngw agutunganye.
