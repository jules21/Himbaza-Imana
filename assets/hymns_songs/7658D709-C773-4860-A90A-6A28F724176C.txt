88.Hazabahw igihe cy'imperuka Urubanza rucibwe na Yesu. Azarucir' abaremwe bose Kuko bazaba bar' imbere ye.
 Nibw' azaherakw abarobanura Nk'umwungeri mu ntama n'ihene: Intama niz' azashyir' iburyo bwe, Ibumos' azahashyir' ihene.

ii.Maze Yes' azabwir' ab' iburyo ati: Ngwino mwahaw' umugisha. Dor' ubwami mwahamagariwe. Uhereye ku kuremwa kw'isi.  Uwo muns' azabashimira cyane. Bazarabagirana nk'izuba. Azabamurikir' Imana Data. Hazabah' umunezero mwinshi.

iii.Azabwira abo b'ibury' ati: Nari nshonje muramfungurira. Mwaranshumbikiye nd' umushyitsi. Mwaranyambitse nambay' ubusa.  Nabo bati: ryari war' umushyitsi, Cyangwa ryari twakubony' ushonje, Ryari twakubonye wambay' ubusa Tugukorer' ibyo byos' uvuze?

iv.Azabwira ab' ibumoso bwe ati: Muv' aho ndi mwabivume mwe, Nimugende mujye mu muriro Wa Satani hamwe n'ingabo ze.
 Ntimwamfunguriy' ubwo nari nshonje. Mfit' inyota ntimwampay' amazi. Nar' umushyitsi ntimwanshumbikira Ntimwansuye ubwo nari ndwaye. 

v. Nuko bazabwir' Umwami Yesu bati: Ryari twakubony' ushonje? Ryari twakubony' ufit’ inyota? Ryari twakubony' ur' umushyitsi?
 Ryari twakubony' urwaye, Mukiza? Ryari twakubonyehw ibyo byose? Ibyo byos' uvuze ntitwabibonye Ngo tubure kukwerek' ineza.

vi. Nuko Yes' azabwir' ab' iburyo, ndets' abwire n'ab' ibumoso bwe ati: Byose mwakorey' abato Burya ni jye mwabikoreraga.  Bose bazibuka ibyo bakoraga Hazarira abo b'ibunoso. Ab' iburyo bazajyanwa mw juru Bazahora banezerw' iteka.
