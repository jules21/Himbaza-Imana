59.Nimuze, mwa ndushyi mwe mwese,Muremerewe, murembye. Yes' aje kubasanganira N'ubuntu bwe n'urukundo Mwese, nimuze ! Muze, mwese ! Mumwitab' arabifuza !

ii.Nubw’ ur' umunyabyaha byinshi, Humura ye ! N' uw'ibambe ! I by' agushakaho ni bike :  N' ukwihan' ukamwizera. Wa munyabyaha we wihebye, Arakogesh' amaraso. 

iii.Umwana wera w'Uwiteka Yubamiy' i Getsemane, Baramubamb' ari tw' azira. Nukw ati : Birarangiye ! Utegerej' iki se, kandi ? Ibyo ko biguhagije !

iv.Dore, yazamutse mw ijuru,Atsinz' urupfu n'ibyaha ! Aratuvugira kuri Se, Agaragaz' amaraso.
 Na S' akareb' izo nkovu ze, Nihanny' akambabarira.

v.Nukw abakijijwe na Yesu Tuzamusanganirayo ! Abo mw isi n'abo mw ijuru Ni ko kuririmba, tuti : Turagushimye ! Haleluya ! Ur' Umukiza n'Umwami !
