328. Yes’ ur' Umukiza wanjye : Kuv' ubu, jye mb' uwawe. Nuhagiwe n'amaraso Yawe, Mwana w' Imama. 

ref:Tugusingize, Mukiza, Yesu, Mwana w'intama. Nuhagiwe n'amaraso : Ngushime, Murokozi 

ii. Kera nararushye cyane Ngo nihesh' amahoro. None ubu, namaramaje Kwirangiriza Yesu.

iii. Nzajya mbwir' abantu bose Agakiza ka Yesu, Gashyitse, katar' igice  N'ubugingo bw'ubuntu. 

iv.Nzajya namamaza Yesu, Nshiz' amanga, nta soni : Uko yambohoy' ingoyi No kunkiz' akankiza. 

v.Mpimbaz' Uwanshunguy' atyo,Ankirish' imbabazi ! Ajy' andind' amp' amahoro : Nukw asingizw' iteka !
