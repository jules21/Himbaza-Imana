195.Yesu yatwise amatabaza ye Yo kumurikir' abo mu mwijima. Tubamurikishiriz' umucyo we, Tubamurikishiriz' ingeso. 

ii.Tur’ amatabaza ya Kristo, Yifuza kureb' uko tumurika; Kand' iyo tuzimy' ababara cyane :Noneho, bagenzi, tumurike ! 

iii.Arashaka kw abandi bareba Kumurika kwacu, bakayoboka. Tumurikir' ab'i wacu n'ahandi,Dushimishe Databuja, Yesu, 

iv.Ni dushaka kumurika dutyo, Twizer' Umukiza wadupfiriye, Tumusab’ Umwuka Wer' atunganye Imitima yacu : tumurike !
