160.Mbeg' urukundo rwaw’umfitiye ! Sinarumenya: rurahebuje.  Mukiza wanjy' umpishurire, Rumpumurize no kunyobora,Ni nteshuka.

ii. Urwo rukundo narugera nte? Ruransumba ; sinarurondora. Njye ndwerekana gus’umfashije, Njye mvug' uko rukiz’abangizi B'abihebe.

iii.Urwo rukundo naruvuga nte, Ko ntabon’amagamb' akwiriye ? Nyamar' akanwa kanjye kifuza Guhora nshimish’Umurokozi Unkund’atyo.

iv.Urwo rukundo narushimira nte Mukiza wanjye, ne guceceka  Ndirimb' imbabazi wangiriye, jy' umunyabyaha wakugomeye, Ukankiza !

v.Nta cyo nabasha kugushimisha; Mukiza wanjye, nd’umukene pe. Niringiy’urukund’umfitiye:Mbes' Uwankunz' akanyitangira,yanyim'iki?

vi.Ndashaka, Yesu, kukugumaho, Ur' isoko y'amaz' ahoraho, Njye mpore nkunywah' ubudasiba, Umar' inyota n'umubabaro, Sinkuveho!
