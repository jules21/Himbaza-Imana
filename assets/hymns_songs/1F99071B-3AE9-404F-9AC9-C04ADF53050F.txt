391.Kugeza gupfa, tuzakuyoboka: Kugeza gupf' ur' Umwami wacu. Duhagaritswe kw ibendera yawe ;  Tuzahapfira tukwiringiye.

ref:Kugeza gupfa, turwan' intambara; Ni kw ingabo z'Umwami zinesha; Kugeza gupf' Umusaraba wawe N' Ibendera yac' abacunguwe.

ii. Kubwawe, Yesu, ni byiza kubaho; Inzira yaw' iratworohera. Ni nde wakwanga kugukurikira,  Wowe, Mukiza wadupfiriye?

iii. Kugeza gupfa, dutegekwe nawe. Ubw' uriho, tuzabaho natwe ! Wumviy' Imana, ntiwanga no gupfa, Ndets' udupfira n'urupfu rubi.

iv.Mukiz' uz' intege nke zacu rwose; tuzi ko twagwa mu nzira yacu, N' utaza gusohoz' isezerano Ryo kuturengera, tukiriho

v.Umwuka waw' udushorer' iteka,Kand' imitima yac' iwumvire, Ngo tub' ubwoko bwaneshej' urupfu Kubwawe, Yesu wadupfiriye!
