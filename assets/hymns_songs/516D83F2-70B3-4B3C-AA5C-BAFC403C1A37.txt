358.Twemere Yesu—Ko yaje mw isi Kutubwir' amahoro ye. Ni We nshuti nziza.—I Gologota,Yahaw' abazimiye.Mpora nizera,—N'ubu nizeye Yuko Yesu yampfiriye; Ku Musaraba,—Yaranesheje; Ambohora mwizeye.

ii.Inzira yanjye—Ni Yesu wanjye;Anjyan' aho yangeneye. N' umugiraneza;—Nta muntu n'umwe Yanga guh' agakiza Mpora nizera, —N'ubu nizeye Yuko Yesu yamfiriye; Ku Musaraba,—Yaranesheje; Ambohora mwizeye.

iii.Mwami w'ubuntu,—Uhimbarizwe Kuk' uturarik' iteka. Tukwitabe twese,—Twumv' ijwi ryawe Rihamagara hose.
 Twizere twese,—Twe guhinyura Ko Yesu yadupfiriye; Ku Musaraba,—Yaranesheje; Abohor' abizeye
