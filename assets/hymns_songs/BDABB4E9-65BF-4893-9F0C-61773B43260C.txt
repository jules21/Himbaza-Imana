390. Ngabo z'umwete, mubyuke, Nimurwanir' Umwami; Mwatoranijwe n'Imana :Kubwayo muranesha.  Mwitegure mu mitima, Mwirind' ibibashika; Isi muyigandurire Yes' Umwami w'abami.

ii.Mubwiriz' ab'isi bose Urukundo rw'Imana; Abatuye mu mwijima Mubabwir' iby'umucyo. Abayobye batizera, Muti : Mwemere Yesu, Kukw akunda guhabura Imitim' izimiye.

iii.Mujye mu bihugu byose,Mubwiriz' iby'Umwami; Mujyan' Umwuka we Wera; Muzavug' ibye neza, I torero rye rigwire Mu bari mw isi yose: Izina ry'Umwami Yesu Ripfukamirwe hose.

iv.Hahirw' abizey' Umwami,Bakamubabarizwa; Bazabon' ingororano Umuns' azagaruka : Baziman' iteka ryose Hamwe n'Umwami Yesu, Mu bwami bwe budashira, Kuko bamukoreye.
