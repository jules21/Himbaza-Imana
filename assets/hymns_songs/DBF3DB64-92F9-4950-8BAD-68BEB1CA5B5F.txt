253. a. Haleluya ! Haleluya ! Haleluya ! 

i.Yesu yarangij' intambara Kuko yanesheje Satani. Tunezerwe, tumusingize !Haleluya ! 

ii.Imbaraga z'urupfu zose Ni We wazinesheje rwose.Tuvuz' impundu, twishimane :Haleluya! 

iii.Kuri wa munsi wa gatatu, Yazuts' afit' icyubahiro; Non'ubu yimanye n'Imana :Haleluya! 

iv.Non' udukiz' ibyaha byacu, N'urubori rw'urupfu na rwo, Tubeho, turirimba, tuti:Haleluya !

b. Haleluya ! Haleluya ! Haleluya ! 

i.Hashimw' Uwatuzukiye ! Kukw avuye mu gituro;Duhuze hamwe,—Twishime tuti: Haleluya ! 

ii.Yesu, waradupfiriye,ub' Umwam' ubahw iteka. Uradukunda;—Uradushaka : Haleluya ! 

iii.Uri mu bwiza bw'ijuru, Uhadutegerereje Udushakisha—Ubuntu bwawe :Haleluya ! 

iv.Tuguh' imitima yacu, Uyitware, Mwami Yesu,Ikwishimire—Iminsi yose : Haleluya !
