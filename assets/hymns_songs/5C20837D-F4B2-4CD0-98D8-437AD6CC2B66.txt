91. Namaze kumeny' ibyiza Byinshi Yesu yakoraga Mu gihe yagenderaga mur' iyi si,
 Aho yageraga hose Niko yajyag' abafasha Nejejwe no kuririmba Yuko Yes' adahinduka. 

ref:Yesu Krist' uko yar' ari Nikw ahor' iminsi yose. Arashak' abazimiye Ndets' akiza n 'abagome Uwo Mukiza ntahinduka.

ii.Kandi hariho n'impumyi Yitwaga Barutimayo. Imaze kumenya ko Yes' ari hafi Yinginga Yesu yizeye Nukw ikizwa kubw' ubuntu.  Nejejwe no kuririmba Yuko Yes' adahinduka.

iii.Yes'ahamagar'abanyabyaha Ndetse n'abarwayi Bos' abahamagarira kubakiza Naw' ukore kunshunda ze Urahabw' imbaraga nshya.
 Ugire nka wa mugore Kuko Yes' adahinduka.

iv.Namaze kumenya yuko Yasabirag' abanzi be. Cyane cyan' ubwo yari ku musaraba N'ukuri yarababajwe Ubwo yambikwag' amahwa.
 Nejejwe no kuririmba Yuko Yes' adahinduka.
