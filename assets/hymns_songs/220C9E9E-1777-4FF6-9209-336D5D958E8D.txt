7.Dor’abera bo mwijuru barishimye cyane Baririmbira Yes’ iyi ndirimbo nshya

ref:Bat’ Icyubahiro cyose kib’ icya Yesu, Wadunz’ akatwuhagiz’ amaraso

ii.Abo bose bari bandujwe n’ibyaha kera none bambay’ imyenday’ abakijijwe bat’ icyubahiro

iii.uwahoze ari umugome Yes’ amuhindura umutambyi w’ umwami tumusingize tut’ icyubahiro

iv.iyo Yes’atadukunda tutarezw’ibyaha  tuba twihebye rwose twakozwa na  non’icyubahiro nde?

v.turirimbire n’ abandi uko yadukijije bibarehereze aho ari,baririmbe bati icyubahiro.
