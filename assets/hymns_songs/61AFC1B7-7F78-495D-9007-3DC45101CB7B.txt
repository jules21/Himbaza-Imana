340. Umutima wanjye wizera Kugusaba, Mana Data; Unyumvane za mpuhwe zawe.Jye umwana wakugomeye. Ni Wowe wenyine nizeye Kw ari Wow' ump' umugisha.

ii.Ndagusanze nguhungiyeho Kukw ari Wowe nizeye. Umpe n'Umwuka wawe wera.Ump' intege ninanirwa, Nimbon' ibyago, nkwizigire K' unyakiran' imbabazi.

iii.ku manywa mba mu mucyo wawe; N'ijoro nkakwegamira; No mu gitondo, nki gusenga,Ujy'untegurir' inzira: No mu maryama, Data mwiza, Undinganiriz' iby'ejo.
