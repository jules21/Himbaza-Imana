
 INDIRIMBO ZO MU GUSHIMISHA IMANA

1.Ur' Uwer' Uwera, Mwami Mana yacu, Kare mu gitondo tukuririmbire : Ur' umunyambarag' ur' umunyebambe; Ur' Ubutat’ uri n'Iman’ imwe.

ii.Ur’ Uwer’ Uwera; dor' abera bose, baguhimbazanya n'abamarayika, Tukuramye Mwami, wahozeho kera, Kand' uriho, kand' uzahoraho.

iii. Ur' Uwer' Uwera, kand' ubwiza bwawe Burut' uko bwarebwa n'abacumuye. Ni wowe wenyine wer' urusha bose Ubuntu no  gutungana kose.

iv. Ur' Uwer' Uwera, Mwami Mana yacu, Ibyaremwe byose biragusingiza : Ur' umunyambarag'ur'umunyebambe: lngoma yawe ntizahanguka.
