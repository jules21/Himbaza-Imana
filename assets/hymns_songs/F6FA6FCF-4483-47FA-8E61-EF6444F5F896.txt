235. Bakristo, nimuze, munezerwe mwese, Nimuze, tugeran' i Betelehemu. Muze kureb' Umwami wahavukiye! 

ref:Nimuze, tumusenge ( x 3) Ni Kristo Yesu. 

ii. Imana Rurema, nubw’ isumba byose, Ntiyanze kubyarwa n'uwo woroheje. ryaturemye, dor’ uko yamanutse ! 

iii. Muririmbe cyane, bamarayika mwese, Mut’ Isumba byos’ ihabw’ icyubahiro, Kand’ amahor’ abe mu bo yishimira ! 

iv. Tuje kukuramya, Mwami Yes’ uvutse, Tuje kugusingiza, Mukiza Yesu, Nubw’ ur’ Imana, none turaturanye.
