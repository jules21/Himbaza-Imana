377. Reka twongere twishimire Rwa rukundo rudahinduka N'amaraso twahongerewe Ku Musarab' i Gologota. 

ref: Twogez' ibya Yesu Tumusingiz' iteka ryose! Twogez' Ibya Yesu, Kugez' ubwo tuzaban' i we 

ii.Muze mwese, mwitabe Yesu, Mwisuke mu maraso none, Mwakir' ubuntu n'imbabazi, Mubiheshwe na za ntimba ze. 

iii.Turi mw isi, tunezererwa Imigisha dusogongeye, Ariko ni tumwirebera, Tuzicwa n'umunezero pe ! 

iv.Tujye twambar' iryo zina rye, Tumukurikire mu nzira.Yes' azaza mu minsi mike; Tuzajya kumusanganira. 

v.Yesu, wez' imitima yacu, Twuzure na bagenzi bacu, Ni ho tuzaba twiteguye Kugusanganir' ugarutse.
