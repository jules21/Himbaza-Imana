157.Mukiz' umpe kumenya,Naho kab' agace, K'urukundo rw'igitangaza Rwatumy' udupfira.  

ref:Umenyeshe, Mukiza, Uko wababajwe, Ubw' Uwera wang' ibyaha pe Wishyiragahw ibyanjye. 

ii.Umenyesh' ibyo, Yesu, Kuko bikomeye, Bikarut'uko byashobora Kumenywa n'umuntu. 

iii.Mbes' imbereri, Yesu, ni zo zakubambye ? Oya si zo, ni za mbabazi Wangiriy' ubwanjye. 

iv.Ntangajwe na rwa rupfu Wamfiriye, Yesu, Ko rwashoboye kunshungura Ngo nkire, Mundane, 

v.Ni rwo runkiz' ibyaha Byose bambonaho; Si byo byonyine: runkuramo N'ibinyihishemo, 

vi.Mukiza, nshenjagura; Men' ibinangiwe! Unyemeze yuk' unesheje : Unyimemo, Mwami.
