Ref:Ndakwizeye, ndakwizeye. ndakwizeye, Mwami Yesu. Ndakwizeye, ndakwizeye, Nizey' iryo jambo ryawe. 

ii.Wihan' ibyo byaha byawe, Wezwe no mu maraso ye. Kand' arakwambik' ukuri Kugez' ubw' azakujyana, 

iii.Imiruho yawe yose, Uyerek' Umwami Yesu. Naho waca mu gicucu Ntabwo Yesu yakureka.
