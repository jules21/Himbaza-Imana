415. Turashim' Imana yuko twavukiye Ku ngoma y'Umwami Yesu, Nyir’ imbabazi. Iyo tuvuka ku ngoma ya Satani, None tuba twihebye, 

ref:Yesu, tugukunde cyane (X 3) Kuko watwitangiye ! 

ii.None baba badutapfunir' itabi, N'amajosi n'amaboko byuzuy' impigi : Byos' Umwami Yesu yabidukuyeho, Nukw aduh' amahoro. 

iii.Dukunde Yes' udukunda turi bato ! Yaturemeshej’ Ijambo ryayo ryonyine; Yaduhaye n'ababyeyi badukunda,Batureran' ineza.
