228. Bahungu, bakobwa,—Nimuze mwese I Betelehemu—Mu ruhongore; Tureb' ibyo Data—Uri mw ijuru Yaduhaye twese—Mur'iri joro ! 

ii. Hamwe n'abashumba,—Dushim’umwami Uryamye mu byatsi—Nk'uworoheje. Dusang’ ababyeyi—Banezerewe; Duhuze n'Intumwa—Kumusingiza. 

iii.Nimupfukamire—Umwami mwiza Uduh’ ubugingo,—Mumuhimbaze ! Muvuze n'impundu—Hamwe n'Intumwa; Mubwire n'abandi—Ibyo mwabonye. 

iv.Twese tumushime,—Wa Mwami mwiza Tumuhimbarize—Kukw adukunda. Dufatanye twese,—Tuvuz’ impundu, Ngw abatamumenye—Baze gukizwa.
