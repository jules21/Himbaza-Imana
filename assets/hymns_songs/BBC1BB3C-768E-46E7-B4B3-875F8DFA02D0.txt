133.Ndagukurikira, Yesu; Nemey' umusaraba.Jye nta shinge, nta rugero : Urampagije wenyine. 

ref:Ndagukurikira, Yesu, Ubwo wanguz’amaraso; Ndakwihaye, Mwami Yesu: Kera waranyitangiye. 

ii.Icyubahiro n'iby'isi, Sinkibimaranira. Kubwa Yesu, ndabiretse: Amp’ibyiza byo mw ijuru. 

iii.Nahw ab'isi bazanyanga, Ni ko bakugiriye. Ntibankund' akaramata: Wowe ntabw' uzanzinukwa. 

iv.Uwansek' ansuzuguye, Byakunsunikiraho. Uko bizamera kose, Uzabinyihanganisha. 

v.Ko mpaw’urukundo rwawe, Har’ikindi nshaka se? Ibinezaneza by'isi, Simbishaka: mfite Yesu.
