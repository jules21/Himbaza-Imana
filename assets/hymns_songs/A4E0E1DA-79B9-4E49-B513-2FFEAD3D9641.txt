57.Yes' araguhamagara :Ngwino, murushywa ! Ubu tugisab' Imana, Ngwino, murushywa ! Ubu n' igihe gikwiye Ngwino, murushywa ! Itaba Yes' uyu munsi ! Ngwino, murushywa !

ii.Ubwo wumv’ uremerewe, Ngwino, murushywa ! Yes’ akuruhure none :Ngwino, murushywa ! Nta w' ajy' ashuka, mugenzi,Ngwino, murushywa ! Yes' arakwakira none : Ngwino, murushywa !

iii.Yes' arakwinginze ng' uze :Ngwino, murushywa ! Aguh' umugisha mwinshi;Ngwino, murushywa ! N' umunyambabazi rwose : Ngwino, murushywa ! Tukigusabir' Imana, Ngwino. murushywa !
