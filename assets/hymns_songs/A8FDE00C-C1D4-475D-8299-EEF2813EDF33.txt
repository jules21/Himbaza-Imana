433.Yemw' abasubir' iyo muva, I kuzimu kwa Nyamutezi, Mukarekur' Umusaraba, Ko muneshejwe ?  

ref:Yes' ubw' azagaruka, Aje kwim' ingoma ve, Tuzamurebesh' amas' as' ate, Dufit' ibyaha? 

ii.Ubw' utihanira gukizwa, Ukaturir' abantu gusa,Ngo bakumenye k' ukijijwe, Urushywa n'iki ? 

iii.Ubw' ur' umuntu w'umwigisha, Wigish' abant' iby'Uwiteka, Ntubageze ku Musaraba, Wigish' iki se ? 

iv.Ubw' ur' umunyangeso nziza, Ushyitsa byose by'Itorero, Ntusohore ku Musaraba, Wizey' iki se ? 

v.Ubw' ur' umunyabwenge bwinshi, Byos' uzi no kubigenzura,K' uyobew' iby'Umusaraba, Bikuvuy' iki ? 

vi.Ubw' ur' umunyesoni nyinshi, Utinya kwihana ng' ukizwe, Wibwira kw ar' uguhemuka, Uzageza he ? 

vii. Ngik' icyambu cy'amaraso ye, Wirohemo, wa mutindi we. He kub' agace wasigaza Katuhagiwe.
