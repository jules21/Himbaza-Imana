287.Yes' azaganz' ibihugu byose,Iyo biva bikagera. Ndetse n'iyi si yos' izab' ubwami bwe, Ari we Mwami w' abami. 

ii.Bazamusenga badacogora;Bazamusingiz' iteka. Izina rye  rizababer' impumuro, Ikwire mw is' isandare. 

iii.Twihute, twamamaz' ibye hose,Mu moko yose yo mw isi, Ngw indimi zose zizavug’ ishimwe rye, Zemere yukw ar' Umwami. 

iv.Ku ngoma ye, n' amahirwe masa: Imboh' arazibobora, Kand' abakene na b' akabakenura, Indushy' akaziruhura

v.Nimuze mwese, mwa byaremwe mwe Mwubah' Umwami w'abami; Namwe bamarayika, turirimbane, Tut' Urakaz' uhimbazwe
