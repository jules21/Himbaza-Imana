130.Nazimiriye kure yawe Non’untarure ! Nagundiriy' ibyaha byanjye : Ndaje, noneho. 

ref:Nditabye  Nditabye Ntabwo nzongera. Nyemerer’ umbabarire. Mwami, nyakira ! 

ii.Napfapfany' iyo myaka yose :Non’untarure  Nkwitwayeho, Mukiza wanjye Ndaje, noneho. 

iii.Narembejwe n’ibyaha byanjye :None untarure! Niringiy’ urukundo rwawe: Ndaje, noneho. 

iv.Wamviriy’amaraso yawe :Non' untarure! Ubwaw' uraz’ uranshungura  Ndaje, noneho. 

v.Umv’uko ntats' unkize, Yesu Non’untarure ! Mukiza, nyeza nk'urubura : Ndaje, noneho.
