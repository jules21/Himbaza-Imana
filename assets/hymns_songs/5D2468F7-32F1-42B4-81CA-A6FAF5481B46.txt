32. Sioni sanganir' Umukwe.Gend' umwerek' umunezero. Wibonez' imbere y'Umukwe. Gir' umwete wo kwitegura. 

Ref:Nezerwa, nezerwa, Umukw'agushakahw impundu. Nezerwa, nezerwa, Ngwin' upfukamir' Ihoraho.

ii.Umukiza wacu yavuye Ku ntebe y'ubwami mw ijuru. Yagaragaye mu ruhinja. Rwaryamishijwe mu muvure. 

iii.Yabay' igitambo gikwiye. Yababarijw' i Gologota. Yapfiriye bose bo mw isi.Duhabw' agakizak' Imana. 

iii.Yaneshej' umwanzi Satani, Ndetse n'urupfu rwaratsinzwe. Yaduhishuriy' ubugingo. Twahawe kuzabahw iteka.

iv.Agenderer' abababaye, Abah' amahoro y'ukuri. Azahora ku ngom' iteka, Kukw ar' Umwam' ukiranuka.
