172.Mwemere kw Iman' ibaha umurokozi , witeguye kubakiza, ubakwiriye.mu ijuru  no mw isi yose, ni We nyir'ububasha;  kand' ubony' uwo Mukiza Aranezerwa.

ii.azatuma muba beza, Mumere nka We; umukurikira wese  Ntabw' azaneshwa. Mu rugamba, ntabahana; Ahor' abarwanira; Kand' ubwe yemeye rwose Kubarengera.

iii.Mukiz' ubwaw' ubabwira Iby'amahoro, Yuk' unyuz' inshuti zawe Mu nzira nziza. Kand' ubwaw' urabarinda Kugira ngo be kugwa,  Bave mu makuba yose Bayanesheje.

iv. Mwam' ibyago ni bitera Cyangw' amakuba, Utwongerer' imbaraga,Tukwiringire. Maze twizigiy' ibyawe, Dutumbire mw ijuru; Twishimire kuzabona Wa munsi mwiza.
