95.Ndashaka kuririmbira Yesu, Yaj' ar' Umwami w'igikundiro. Yarababajwe ndetse no gupfa, Kugira ngw ambature mu byaha. 

ref:Nzahora ndirimbira Yesu Kubw' urukundo yangiriye, Yanyishyuriy' imyenda yose, Yarambohoye muri byose. 

ii.Nd uwo guhamy'iby'urwo rukundo rwabonetse mu Mukiza wanjye, Cyane cyan' uko yaj' akankiza Kandi nar' uwo kurimbuka. 

iii.Ndashimir' uwo Mukiza wanjye, Kubw' ububasha bwe butangaje. Kandi niw' unshyiramw imbaraga Zo kunesh' umwanzi Satani. 

iv.Ndashaka rwose kuririmbira Uwo Mwami wanjye, Yesu Kristo. Koko n'ukuri yarankijije. Nzahora mpirw' iminsi yose.
