33.Ndagushimiye, Mukiza,Urukundo wankunze; Nuk' umpindur’ umugende  W'amazi y'ubugingo.

Ref:Mb' umugende gusa, Yesu,Wuzuyemw imbaraga Z'agakiza kawe, Mwami, ubone kuntembamo 

ii.Mb' umugend' ugez' amazi Mu mitim’ isaraye, Ibone kunyw' agakiza K'ubutumwa bw'ineza.

iii.Mb' ikibindi cyawe, Yesu :Nuk' unyoz' untunganye, Maz' unyuzuz' ayo mazi, Niyo Mwuka w'Imana.

iv.Mb' umugabo wo guhamya Uburyo wankijije : Waranguze ngo mb' uwawe, Nuk' unyuzure rwose.

v.Yesu, suk' Umwuka Wera Mu mutima wanjy’ ubu, Kugira ngo ngez' amazi Yawe mu basaraye !
