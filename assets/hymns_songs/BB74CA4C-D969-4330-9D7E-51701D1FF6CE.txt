102.iby' isi Yesu yabinkuyemo : ubw' amaraso, ntibinkang' ubu. nunamy' imbere y'Umusaraba; Yesu yanyejeje de mu maraso ye. 

ref:Jye nkunda... jye nkunda...Umukiza wanjye Wanyiguriy' i Gologota ku Giti. 

ii.Nari nazimiriye mu byaha, Nd' imbata y'ishyari n'inzangano, Umunsi w'amateka nywutinya, Ankirish' amaraso ku Musaraba. 

iii.Jye nar' imbat' amp' umudendezo;  Jye nar' impumyi, arampumura; Jye nari  intumbi, maz' aranzura ! Nimwumv' ayo mahoro Yesu yampaye!
