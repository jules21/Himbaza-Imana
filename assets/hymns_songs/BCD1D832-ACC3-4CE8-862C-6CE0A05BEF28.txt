65.Musange Umwami ugira ubuntu ,abatakimwizigira ntiyanga kwakira abatindi;ahabura abazimiye Kandi yemeye kubapfira: Yabaviriy' amaraso. Nta kindi abasha gukirisha Abanze kumvir' Imana.

ii.Musang' Umwami ujy' ahembura Nimuze, mwes' abaguye, Batacyizigir' agakiza :Abategey' amaboko !
 Yakirishij' ubuntu bwinshi Wa munyabyahakaz' arira; Na Wa mwambuzi babambanywe Ntiyamwisungiy' ubusa.

iii.Musang' ubasha kuruhura; Az' ibibarushya byose. Kubw' urukundo rusaga, Ahez' ibibashavuza. Cya gihe,  muri Getsemani,Yariz' amarira menshi : Kubw' ubuntu bwe buhoraho, Azahanagur' ayanyu.
