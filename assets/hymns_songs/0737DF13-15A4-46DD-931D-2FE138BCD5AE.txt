255. Yesu Kristo yarazutse : Twese tumushime : Haleluya Uyu munsi, tunezerwe,Twese tumushime : Haleluya  Kera ku Musaraba, Ni ho Yesu yatubabarijwe, Ngw ab' impongano y'ibyaha byacu : Haleluya, reka tumuhimbaze 

ii.Yes’ Umwami wo mw’ Ijuru, Twese tumushime:Haleluya  Kukw ar’ umukiza w’abo mw’isi,Yarabambw’arahambwa  Ajya n’i kuzimu mu bapfuye.Maz’arabinesh’ava mu rupfu Haleluya, reka tumuhimbaze 

iii.Ibyo yababajwe byose, Umukiza wac' abishimirwe Ni byo byonyine byatumye Tugir' ubugingo budashira. None, yimye mw ijuru, Ahw abamarayika baririmba, Tumusingizanye na bo, tuti : Haleluya, reka tumuhimbaze.
