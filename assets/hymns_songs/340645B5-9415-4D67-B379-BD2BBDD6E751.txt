47.Ube maso, Mukristo: Nturuhuke nonaha, Amakub'ari hafi; Usabe !

ii.Ababisha b'lmana Bajya bakwubikira, Baguciriy' ibico; Usabe !

iii. Ujye wambar’ intwaro yesu yakwihereye. Ku manywa na n’ijoro, Wirinde !

iv. Umv' abatsinze kera iyo ntambar' urwana; Baraguhugura, ngo : Usabe !

v.Umva n'Umwami wawe, Yesu wagupfiriye, Ni We watubwiy' ati ; Ba maso !

vi.Iryo jamb'uryemere N'umutima wizeye, Kugira ng' urikore; Usabe !
