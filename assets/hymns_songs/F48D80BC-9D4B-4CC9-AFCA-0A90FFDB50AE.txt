191.Nunguk' ubuntu bwa Yesu, Menye n'imbabaz' afite, Nunguk' ubwenge bw'ukuri, Meny' urukundo yankunze !Nunguk' ibya Yesu ! 

ref:Nunguk' ibya Yesu  Nunguk' ubwenge bw'ukuri,  Meny' urukundo yankunze. 

ii.Nshishikarir' ibya Yesu, Meny' iby' anshakaho byose. Mwuka Wer' umfuturire : Mbwir' uko Yesu yankunze. 

iii.Nunguk' ijambo rya Yesu, Mbane n'Umwam' antoneshe; Numvire byos' antegeka, Mpuze na We, muyoboke. 

iv.Meny’ iby'ubwami bwa Yesu, Menye n'ubwiza bwe bwose, Meny' ukw azim' ingoma ye, Ari We Mwami w'abami.
