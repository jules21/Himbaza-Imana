412.Isi yos' iririmbe,— ibitangaza Umukiza wa twese—Yadukoreye.Amahang' ukw angana Yos' asingize Yesu ! 

ii.I burasirazuba,—Bamuhimbaze ! Abashinwa bashime—Umucunguzi,Abahindi bemere Yesu wabapfiriye. 

iii.Kandi n'Abisilamu—Bamusingize : N'amahanga barimo—Ayabature.Yes' Umwami, bemere Yuko yabacunguye. 

iv.Abatuye mu mbeho,—Aho bagira Ijoro rirerire—Ritinda gucya,Bemer' Umucyo w'isi, Ngo bave mu mwijima. 

v.N'abo mu Rwanda rwacu,—Bamusingize.Bang' ingeso za kera,—Bamuyoboka, Bati : Ngwin' utwimemo, Ur' Umwami w'ijuru. 

vi.Amahanga yo mw isi—Yose ukw angana Yemer' Umucunguzi,—Amuyoboke;Abakijijwe bose Bamusingiz' iteka.
