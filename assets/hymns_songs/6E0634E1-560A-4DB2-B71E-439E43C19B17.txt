250.Shimwa,Mwami Yesu,—Ko wamviriye Amaraso yawe—Ku Musaraba. 

ii.Mur' ayo maraso,—Ni mo mbonera I neza n'ubuntu—No gusugira. 

iii.Umugezi wayo—Ni w' uboneza Abawibiramo—Ntibarimbuke. 

iv.Ibuk' ay'Aberi—Ko yatakiye Imana ngw ihore—Uyavushije. 

v.Nyamar' aya Yesu—Ajy' ansabira Uwitek’Imana—Kumbabarira. 

vi.Iy’aminjagiwe—K’uwacumuye, Satan’ aratinya,—Agahamuka ! 

vii.Non’abari mw isi—Tuyasingize ! Namwe mwikirize,—Bamarayika ! 

viii.Ab’Imana twese—Turangurure, Dushim’ amaraso— Yaducunguye !
