351.Yemw' abahung' Umukiza,Ni nd' ubirukana se  Ariko ni We Mushumba Udushak' intama ze. 

ii.Nkuk' umwunger' ajy' ashaka iyazimiye yose, Uko ni ko Yesu yaje Ngw adupfir' intama ze. 

iii.Mudatiny' ibyaha byanyu :Amaraso ya Yesu, Ayamenekeye twese; Ni y' abyeyura byose ! 

iv.Naho byaba byinshi bite, Bikabaremera, Umukiza wacu Yesu Ntazabyibuk' ukundi. 

v. Yemw' abifuz' ubugingo Butazashir' iteka, Ntimugir' undi musanga : Yes'arabiteguye ! 

vi.Mwe gukund' ibyaha byanyu Kubirutisha Yesu, Kuko byatumy' adupfira, Ngo tubane mw ijuru.

vii.Muzahabw' ibyiza byose Yadusezeranije, No kudapf' iteka ryose : Muze, mwe gucikanwa  

viii.Ni Satani waduhumye :Twabay' ibiretwa bye. Noneho, turamucitse; Twitaby' Umucunguzi. 

ix.Yes' Umwami yatubwiye at' Abaremerewe N'abarushye, muz' aho ndi, Mbaruhure by' ukuri ! 

x.Mwami Yesu, sinz' umunsi Uzanjyana mw ijuru. Icyo nzi n' ukuba maso, Ng' unsange niteguye  

xi.Ubwo tuzager' i Wawe,Tuzanezerwa cyane, Twicaranye nawe, Yesu, Hamwe n'abakijijwe. 

xii. Mwami Yesu, singitinze ; Ubu dore, nditabye. nejejwe n'ubwami bwawe; Tuzabane mw ijuru !
