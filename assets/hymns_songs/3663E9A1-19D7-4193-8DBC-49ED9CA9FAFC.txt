105.Ubuntu bw' Imana—Butangaje Ni bwo bwankuyeho—Imigozi.Byavuye se kuki ? Byavuye ku gushaka Kw'Imana yonyine—Yambohoye. 

ii.Ni Yesu wenyine—Wantoreye Guhirwa mw’ ijuru—Mu bwami bwe.Mwese nimumenye Yuk’Umukiza wacu Ajy'asanganira—Abaz' i We. 

iii.S' uko nari mwiza—Haba na mba ! N' ubuntu bwa Yesu—Yangiriye.Yarambabarijwe Kuri wa Musaraba Ndetse yabambanywe—N'abambuzi. 

iv.Kand' iyo nibutse—Ibya Yesu, Uko yagiriwe—Bamubamba,Binyibuts' ibyaha ; We nta kibi yakoze; Ni jyewe yazize :—N' Umukiza ! 

v.Ub' icyo nifuza —N'ukuguma Mu Mukiza wanjye,—Mwihishemo. Ni We ngabo yacu Ihor' idukingira Imyambi wa Mubi—Aturasa. 

vi.Non' Icyo ngusabye,—Mwami Yesu : Ingeso wa Mubi —Yanyanduje Zashaka kubyuka, Zirimbur' uzice pe, Mpore nihariwe—N'ibya Yesu. 

vii.None, maso yanjye,—Na we matwi, Nawe munwa wanjye,—Mubiri we !Mwese, mushimishe Uwankuye mu ngoyi. Ibyaremwe byose,—Tumushime
