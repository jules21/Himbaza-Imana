177.Mwen' Imana yizerwa,Waterwa n'ubwoba ? Mbega, ntiwatorewe Umwam' ugukunda ? Ng' umeny' Uwagutoye, Uce mu nzira ye, Unyuz' ikirenge mu cye Kukw ari we Shobuja.

ii.N’ ubabara, wibwire Kw akubabarira; Nihagir' ugutuka, Umubabarire; Wizigir' ugukunda :Shikama kigabo,  Ng' uzatsind' abanzi bawe, Uberek' urukundo.

iii.N' ubon' ibiruhanya,Ukumva k’ utsinzwe, We kugir' agahinda, Meny' ibigufasha; Ngibi : senga, wizere; Komeza gusenga; Maz' ureb' Umusaraba W'Umwana w'Uwiteka.
