137.Wowe, Mana, ndaguhimbaza Kukw ari nta yindi man’ibaho; Nguhaye n'umutima wanjye Ump' imbaraga z'Umwuka wawe, Mbashe gukor' ibikunezeza, Mw izina ry'Umwami wacu Yesu.

ii.Nuzuzw’Umwuka wawe wera, Umutima wanjy' uwutegeke; Ujy'unyobor inzira yawe, Ngo nyinyuremo ntajya nzimira; Kandi, ni nanirw' ujy'untabara, Ugez’ubw’uzansohoz’i Wawe.
