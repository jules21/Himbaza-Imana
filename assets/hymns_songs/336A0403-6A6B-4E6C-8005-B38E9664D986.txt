117.Numvise Yes'ambwir'ati:ngwino nkuruhure.zana ibyo bikuruhije,mbikwikorerere! musang'uko nari nd'uko;ndushye mfite ishavu;ambera uburuhukiro,ampa umunezero.

ii.numvise Yesu ambwira ati:nguherey'ubuntu amaz' azan' ubugingo,unyw' uram' iteka.njy'aho ari,nywa nezerewe kuri ayo mazi ye umutima ushira inyota,mpabwa ubugingo bwe.

iii.numvise Yesu ambwira ati :ni jye mucyo w'isi.wavukiye mu isi,wavukiye mu mwijima,ngwin'umurikirwe. ndebye kuri Yesu mbona ko ari izuba ryanjye rizamvira iminsi yose y'ubugingo bwanjye.
