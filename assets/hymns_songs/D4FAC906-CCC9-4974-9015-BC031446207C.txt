30.Ai Mukunzi wanjye we, Umpe kukwihishamo. iyo ntewe n'iby'isi N'ingabo za Satani, Mutabaz’umpish' ubwo
, Babanzi be kumfata Umber' ubuhungiro, Mu rupf' uzanyakire.

ii.Nta bundi buhungiro, Mwam’ unyirokorere; We kunsiga, Krisito, Komez' uiy' undengera. Ni Wow' ump’ imbaraga,
 Nta wundi mfit' umfasha; Mbundikiz' amababa, Uhor' undind' ibibi.

iii.Ur' umunyambabazi Zitarangira, Yesu; Usindagiz' urushye, N'urway' uramukiza,  Urera bihebuje,
Ntabwo ngutunganiye; Ndi mubi bikabije,  Wow' urakiranuka.

iv.Yesu ni Wowe soko Y'amazi y'ubugingo, Reka nkunyweho cyane, Undudubiz' imbere. Nuk' umutima
 wanjye Ujye wuzur' iteka, Amazi y'iyo soko Abe mensh' adakama
