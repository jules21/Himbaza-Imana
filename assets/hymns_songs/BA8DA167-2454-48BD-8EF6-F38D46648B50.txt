212 .a. Mw ijuru, hariho—isi nziza; Abera ba Yesu—Ni ho bari. Bishimira cyane Kumuhimbaz’ iteka Kandi  basingiza— izina rye. 

ii. Mwa banyabyaha mwe,— mutebuke Kuz' aho Yes’ ari Ntimutinde! Ubwo tuzabana Nawe, Yesu, mw’ ijuru  Tutanduye rwose, —Tuzishima.

iii.Hahirw’ ababayo, Kukw’ Imana Ijy'ibanezeza—Bihebuje. Dutanguranirwe Ah’ ubwami bwa Yesu Buri,  n'ubwiza bwe—Budashira.

b. Ni mw’ ijuru ku Mana —Abizeye Bazateranirizwa— Mu mahirwe. Kubw’Umwuka w’ Umwami Bateraniyeyo.Bazerura ngo, Shimwa—Shimwa,Mwami!

ii. Umwana w’iman’ubwe,—yavuyeyo   Ngw ajy'atunyuza neza— Mu nzira ye. Tujye twibuk'iteka Kw’ ari W' utwigisha;Tuvuge tuti: Shimwa Shimwa,Mwami!Mwami!

iii.Twebwe twes’ ab'iyi si,—Tumushake Na bene wacu bose—Tubijyane. Tunezerwe, twizere, Twebw' abacunguwe,  Twerure tuti: Shimwa,—Shimwa!Mwami!
