363.Jye mpisemo Yesu; murutish' iyi si yose; Andutir' amafaranga cyangw' izahabu. Jye nkund' Uwabambwe, murutisha n'amatungo. Andutira n'amasambu n'amazu meza.

Ref:Uwanyimika, nkab' Umwami w'ishyanga rinini rite, Nd’imbata y'icyaha cyose, sinabikunda. Ahubwo niragije Yes' Umukunzi wanjye : Sinkita ku biri mw Isi: Yes' aranyuze !

ii.Jye mpisemo Yes' Umucunguzi wanjy' unkunda; Andutira no gushimwa n'ab'isi bose. Umv’ icyo nifuza :  sinshaka kumenyekana; Njye nyobok’ Umwami Yesu iminsi yose. 

iii. Andyohera cyane : nta buki buryoha nka We; Ni mwiza; n' uw'igikundiro cyinshi cyane; Ahumura neza kurush' ibirabyo byose : Nabur' iki se, mufite, kw andangirije?
