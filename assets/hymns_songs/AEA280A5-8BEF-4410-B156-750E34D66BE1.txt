112.Nzi k' Umukiz' ankunda : Niko tubibwirizwa. Abato bose n'abe, Kandi n' uw'imbaraga.

ref:Yes' arankunda (*3) Mpora mbihamya ntyo.

ii.Yesu n' uw'imbaraga : Yapfiriye kunkiza.Ni mwizer’ azanjyana Mu bwami bwo mw ijuru.

iii.Arankund'  andindira mumigendere yanjye; ngo njye nishingikiriza mu muryango we wera.
