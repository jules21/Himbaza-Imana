ii. Ntugir’ icyutinya: Umucunguz’ ariho Wizer’ ukomeje:Arakurengera Utowe na Yesu: witiny’ aragushaka;Yarakwishyuriye Umwami yazutse 

iii. Twebw’ abacunguwe Tuzava mu bituro; Azadukangura, Aduh' ubugingo. Kandi hamwe na We Tuzicara kwa Data Mu bwiza bw'iteka. Umwami yazutse

iv. Noneho, nimuze : Twih' Umukiza wacu Dukundane nka We, Tub' abera nka We. Ubwo turi mw isi,Tumwigane, tubeho, Turindir' ijuru. Umwami yazutse
