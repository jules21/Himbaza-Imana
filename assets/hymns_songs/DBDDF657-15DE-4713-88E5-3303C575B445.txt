37.Nubw' ugeze mu magorwa, wizere, wizere. Iman' izabitunganya nib' uyizeye. 

Ref:Niba wizey' Ihoraho, Izabigufashamo. Izakor' ibitangaza, Niba wizeye.

ii.Nib' ufit' umubabaro, wizere, wizere. Uzahozwa n'lhoraho, Niba wizeye. 

iii.N'ubon' ibigerageza, wizere, wizere. Wemere yuk' ubinesha, Niba wizeye. 

iv.Nib' uzi k'uri wenyine, wizere, wizere. Yes' azab' akuri hafi, niba wizeye. 

v.Nib' urushye mu rugendo, wizere, wizere. Niy' izatungany' inzira, nib' uyizeye.
