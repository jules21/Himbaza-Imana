24. Njya nshima nshima nshima nshim' Imana mu mutima, Mu mutima, mu mutima. Njya nshima nshima nshima nshim’ Imana
 mu mutima, Mu mutima wanjye.

ii.Nta n'urubanza, nta rubanza nshiriweho rwose, Mu mutima, mu mutima. Nta n'urubanza, nta rubanza nshiriweho
 rwose,Mu mutima wanjye.

iii.Mfit' amahor' arut' uko yamenywa mu mutima, Mu mutima, mu mutima. Mfit' amahor' arut' uko yamenywa
 mu mutima, Mu mutima wanjye.
