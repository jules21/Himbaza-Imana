271.Ubw' impanda z'Uwiteka zizavuzwa mw ijuru, Habay’urya munsi mwiz' uhebuje, Abakijijwe bo mwisi bose bazamwitaba,Abakoranirije mw ijuru rye.

ref:Ubw' aza... z' ahamaga.. .ra, Amazi.. .na y'abe bo.. .se, Mu Gita.. .bo cy'ubugi.. .ngo, Nzi ko nzumv' izina ryanjye, nkitaba.

ii.Uwo munsi, hazazurw' abapfiriye muri We, Bave mu bituro nk'Umucunguzi. Bazahurira mw ijuru n'abazaba bariho . Nzaba mpari nanjye—sinzaburayo 


iii.Yesu. Databuja, nzamukorera, ntacogora:Nkwiz' ubuntu n'urukund' amfitiye.Maz' imirimo yo mw is' ajy'ampa,ni nyirangiza.Azampamagar’ angeze mw ijuru.
