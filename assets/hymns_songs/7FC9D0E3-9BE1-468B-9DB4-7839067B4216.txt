 43.Ngwino, Mwami Yesu, ngwino, Kuko tugukumbuye. Utuvane mu ntege nke, Uturuhure vuba ! 

ii.Yesu, byishimo by'abawe,Byiringiro by'ababi, Amahanga yose yose. Arakwifuza cyane.

iii.Yesu ni ryo zina wiswe :Waje kuturokora, Wavuts’ ur' akana gato, Nyamar' uri n'Umwami.

iv.Kubw’ Umwuka wawe wera, Mwami Yes' unyimemo ! Nuk' ungirir' uk' ushaka, Uzangeze mw ijuru !
