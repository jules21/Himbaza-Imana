34.Ndusheho kukwegera, Mukiza, Ne kukuba kure: Nkwishingikirizeho, Mukiza, Ungendeshe neza.

ii.Nerekan’ icyubahiro cyawe Mu byo nkora byose; Akanwa kanjye kajye kavuga Ibyiz' umbwiriza.

iii.Umpindure nk'isoko y'ibyiza,Itemb' idakama; He kugir' undi nkorer' iteka, Databuja mwiza.

iv.Kand' imigambi yanjye n'inama Ubyigandurire; Nyemeza k' ur' Umwami wenyine Untegeka byose.

v.Ni Wowe mutsima w'ubugingo N'amazi mazima; iyo nishwe n'inzara n'inyota, Ni Wow' ujy' umpaza.

vi.Ni Wow' uhaza kwifuza kwiza,Kose niyumvamo. Ni tujy' i wawe, ntacyo, Mukiza, Tuzifuz' ukundi.
