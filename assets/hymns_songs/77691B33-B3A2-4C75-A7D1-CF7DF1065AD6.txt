262. Umva gusaba kwanjye, Mwuka w'imbaraga  Shyir' ububasha bwawe Mu mutima wanjye. Mwuka Wera, hishura ibyaha bindimo; Mpesh' ubugingo bushya, ibyaha bimvemo. 

ii.Za mu mutima wanjye Mbese, nabaho nte, Ntagufite ng' umbemo? Najya nizera nte ? Mwuka, nyobora neza :Nzajya nkwemerera; Ump' ubugingo bushya N'umutima mwiza.

iii.Ne kongera gutinya iby'Umwanzi mubi, Kuk' ukunda kumfasha,Ukampa kunesha. Mwuka, nyemez' ibyawe Mu mutima wanjye; Uwuzuz' ubugingo No kunesha byose
