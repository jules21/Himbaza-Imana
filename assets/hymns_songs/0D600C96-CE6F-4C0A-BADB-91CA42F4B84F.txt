347.Yesu nguriy' ahw ahagaze; Dor' ukw aboshywe n'abanzi be  Umva Pilat' arabaz' ati :Yesu mugirire nte ?
 
ref:Uramugenza s'ute ?hitamo nonaha  Naw' utazibaz' ubundi, Uti  Arangenz' ate ? 

ii.Ibyo na non' ubihitemo ! Wabyemera, wamuhinyura, Cyangwa wamwimika wishimye.Uramugirir' ute ?

iii. Ubwo Pilato yashakaga Kwirengagiza guhitamo, Byaramunaniye : naw' ibyo Ntibyagushobokera. 

iv.Petero yaramwihakanye; Ni ko guhitamo nabi pe ! Wowe s' ahubwo ntiwakunda Gupfa bamuguhora ? 

v.Yes' umusubize nez' uti :Nd' uwawe, Mwami, ndakwihaye; Umpe kujy' aho wanyobora, Nishimiy' iby' ukunda !
