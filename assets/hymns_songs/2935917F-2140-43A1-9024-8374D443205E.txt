231.Umwana yavukiye  Mu murwa wa Dawidi Ni we Yes' Uwiteka Yadusezeranije. 

ref:Duhimbaz' uwo munsi Yesu yatuvukiye; Tubyamamaze hose Yuko haj’ umukiza 

ii.Habayehw’abungeri,Babona marayika, Baratinya; naw’ ati : Noneho, mutinyuke ! 

iii.Ati : Krist’ Umukiza Yaje mu gihugu cye; Umuremyi wa byose Yavukiye mw’ icumbi ! 

iv. Mwana wera, w' Imana, Turagupfukamiye, Tugushimira cyane Yuk’ utujemo, Yesu !
