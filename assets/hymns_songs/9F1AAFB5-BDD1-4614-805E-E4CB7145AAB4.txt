281.Ngiy' inkuru twumvise ;N'uko Yes' akiza! Vuga hos' iyo nkuru Yuko Yes' akiza ! Menyesh' abarimbuka,  Abo mu birorero N'abo mu midugudu, Yuko Yes' akiza !

ii.Menyesh' abananiwe Yuko Yes' akiza Menyesh' abashobewe Yuko Yes’ akiza!  Yavuy' amaraso ye Kera ku Musaraba,
 Ngw abakize, be gupfa:  Ni ko Yes’ akiza !

iii.I Rukig' ubabwire Yuko Yes' akiza ! Umvish' i Bugesera Yuko Yes' akiza ! Vug' i Bunyambiriri N'i Ndorwa n'i Bumbogo,
 Bwira n'i Bufumbira Yuko Yes' akiza !

iv.Banyarwanda, mumenye Yuko Yes' akiza ! Nimwihane, mwemere Yuko Yes' akiza ! Umvish' abacuruzi, No mu nkik' ubivuge,
 N’ i buzungu n'i bwami, Yuko Yes' akiza !
