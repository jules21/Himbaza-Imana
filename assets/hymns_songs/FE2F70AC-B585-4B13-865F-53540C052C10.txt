302.Mukiza, Mwungeri mwiza, Uturinde tw' abana bawe; Iri jor' utube hafi, Ngo turamuk' amahoro.

ii.Wiriw’ uturinze hose, Uturagiy' intama zawe; Kandi wadutunze neza : Nuko, wumv' ibyo dusaba.

iii.Yesu, Mwunger' udukunda, Tubabarir' ibyaha byacu; Rind' abo dukunda bose; Nyum' uzatugez' i Wawe!
