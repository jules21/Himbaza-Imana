268.Ubw' Umwami Yes' azaza Kwend' abo yatoye, Ab' akunda b'abatoni, Bamuyobotse. 
		   
ref:Bazaka nk'inyenyeri Zitatse kw ikamba rye; Bazatangaza cyane, Bakize rwose.
