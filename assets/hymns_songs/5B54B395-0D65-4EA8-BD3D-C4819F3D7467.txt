143.Yesu, ko wankunze, bigatum’ umfira.None nakwim' iki ? Ndakwihay’ubu, Umutima wanjye n'ibyo mfite
 byose Non’ ubyiharire : sinkir’uwanjye.

ii.Uhagaz’imbere y'intebe y'ubuntu, Itek’umvugira k’Uwiteka, So. Mwam' ujy’ unshoboza, ntwar’umusaraba. Nkwize n'imbabazi z'Uwanshunguye.

iii.Mwami, ndakwinginga : umpe gusa nawe :Mpa kugushimisha, kand’unyigishe. Nige kugukunda;  nige kukumvira, Nshak' abazimiye, mbakugezeho!
