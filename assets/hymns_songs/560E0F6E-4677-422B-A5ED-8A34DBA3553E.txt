ii.Ariko wa Mwana W'Intama w'Imana Ni We wenyin' ushobora Kudukiz' ibyaha. 

iii.Yabay'igitambo Kiruta bya bindi; Nta yandi maras' ariho Yo kutwoz' ibyaha. 

iv.Ndambits' ikiganza Ku mutwe wa Yesu, Mushyizehw’ ibyaha byanjye, Mbyatuye nihannye.
