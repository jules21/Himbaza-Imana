62.Ntumwa za Yesu, murangurure, Mwamamaze mw is' ubwo butumwa bwe !Nimukwiz' inkuru z'Umucunguzi : Ubikunz' amwitabe ! 

ref:haz' uwemeye ! Haz' uwemeye  Mubibwir' abato n'abakomeye. Yemera gutarur' abazimiye : Ubikunz' amwitabe! 

ii.Ubikunze naze, ntakererwe. Yinjire mw irembo twugururiwe. Yesu ni we nzir' igeza mw ijuru :Ubikunz' amwitabe ! 

iii.Ubikunze wese namwumvire : Ntabw' avuguruz' isezerano rye. Umusang' agabany' ubugingo bwe :Ubikunz' amwitabe !
