113.Nkunda kwizigira Yesu,Nkajya mwumvira rwose, Ngo njye nishingikiriza Ku masezerano ye.

ref:Yesu, Yesu, ndakwizeye: Ur' uwo kwiringirwa  Yesu, Yesu mwiza nkunda, Ndusheho kukwizera.

ii.Nkunda kwizigira Yesu :Nkizwa n'amaraso ye. Nishyize mu ruzi rwayo, Nogamo, ndatungana.

iii.Nkunda kwizigira Yesu,Nkaruhuk' intambara Y'ibicumuro n'ibyaha, Akamp' umunezero.

iv.Mpimbawe n'uko nkwizeye,Mutabaz' undengera; Nizeye k' umpora hafi; iteka tugumane
