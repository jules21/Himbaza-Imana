
104. Yesu, ni wowe mucyo, Ni wowe nshima kuko wankunze. Mwami, byose wakoze Kutwitangira ndabigushima. 

ref: Yesu, ur' amahoro yacu, Kuko watuberey' inshungu.  

ii.Mwami, ndanezerewe kukuririmba kuko wankunze. Yesu, ko mez' umfashe, Nanjye ngukunde, nkuririmbire. 

iii.Yesu, ngwino tubane, ngwin' unyobore mu nzira yawe. Kandi ni wowe nzira, ur' ubugingo ndetse n'ukuri. 

iv. Nziko har' igihugu cy' umucyo mwinshi ku bakijijwe. Ndetse icyo gihugu N'icy' izahabu zitatse neza.
