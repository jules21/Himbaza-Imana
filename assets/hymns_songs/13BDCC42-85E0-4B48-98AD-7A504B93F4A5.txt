56.Yes' aragutegereza; N' inshuti yawe. Araguhamagar' ati : Ngwino hano !

ii.Ita ku magambo meza Akubwir' ati : Nabambiwe kugira ngo Ngucungure.

iii.Mbes’ ubuntu bwanjye bwinshi Ubwangir' iki ? Ntiwanyemera, nkaguha Agakiza ?

iv.Mbese ntiwamaz' imyaka myinshi mu byaha, Ur' imbata ya Satani, Nturambiwe ?

v.None, reka nkubohore, Naw' unyizere, Unkurikire wishimye, Ub' uwanjye.

vi.Yesu yahoz' akubwira Kensh' ibyo byiza; Dore, n'ub' arakwinginga ! Wamwang' ute ?

vii.We gutind' ahar' ijwi rye Ryahora vuba; Ubu n'igihe gikundwa, We gutinda.

viii. Mwami Yesu, singitinda; Noneho ndaje. Ububata bwawe bwiza Ni bwo nkunze.
