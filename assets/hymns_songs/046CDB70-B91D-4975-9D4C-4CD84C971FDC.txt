434.Abo mur'iyi si mbi Bifuza kwinezeza, Kand' iteka bifuza Icyubahiro. 

ref:Twebwehw abakijijwe Tubaburire  

ii.Bishwe n'Umurimbuzi, Abagir' imbata ze, Yanga ko bameny' uko Yes' abatura. 

iii.Igomwa rya Satani, Ajya yoshy' abantu be Abahemukir' atyo, Akabata pe. 

iv.Naho bamutakira, Ntabwo yabarengera; No ku munsi w'imanza Ntazabafasha ! 

v.Ajy'ashuk' abant' atyo, Ngw abate mu muriro, Ngw atabon' iryo shyano,Ari wenyine. 

vi.Mana, turakwinginze, Ubahumur' amaso, Ngo bahung' umuriro, Bagutakire ! 

vii.Wa Mukiza wacu we, Umva, turakwinginga : Ngwin' utwigishe neza Kubaburira.
