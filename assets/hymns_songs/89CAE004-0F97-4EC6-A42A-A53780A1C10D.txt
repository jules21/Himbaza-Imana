285.Kiz' abarimbuka; renger' abapfa; Bakiz' ibyaha n'urupfu rubi! Shak' abazimiye; byuts' abaguye; Menyesha bose ko Yes' akiza. 

ref:Kiz' abarimbuka, renger' abapfa, Bageze kwa Yesu, ngw’ abakize ! 

ii.Abamushungera, na bo ntabanga;Ategerej' abihana neza. Bahate bihane, bizere Yesu. Ujy'ubasabira ngo bakizwe. 

iii.Toz' abazimiye, be kuzuyaza, Barek' irari ridakwiriye : Non' ubakangure, bisubiremo, Bagarukir' Uwabacunguye. 

Iv.Kiz' abarimbuka : yaragutumye,Kand' akubashish' uwo murimo. Bos' ubayobore mu nzira ntoya! menyesh' indushyi ko zaruhuwe !
