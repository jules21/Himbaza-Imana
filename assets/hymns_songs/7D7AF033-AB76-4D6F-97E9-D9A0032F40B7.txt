366. Ngutuy' ubugingo bwanjye, Yesu, Mwana w' Imana; Ubwakire n'urukundo, Ngo bub' ubwaw' iteka.Nkuzaniy' ibyaha byanjye N'umutim' unangiwe : Mu maraso wamviriye, Unyoze, Mucunguzi.

ii.Uz' uko mpora nirwanya Ngo nkomez' umutima; Uz' uko nari merewe N'ishavu n'agahinda. Ububasha bwawe. Mwami, Bundokore mu byaha Kand' Umwuka w'ubugingo Ujy' unkura mu bibi.

iii.Nemeye kwitanga rwose,Nta cyo nishigarije; Mpisemo kujyana nawe No kukumvira, yesu. Njye nkuyobokan' ishimwe Kukw ari wow' ubimpa; Ujy' unyobor' inzir' ijya Mu bwami bwawe bwiza.

iv.Reb' uyu muhigo mpize Nibwo nzivuguruza. Wukomeresh' amaraso N'urukundo wankunze. Itek' iyo nirebyeho, Ndiheba, nkazimira; Ariko kuk' ujy'unkunda, Uzamfasha, Mukiza.
