29.Uduhagarare Hagati, Mukiza,Iki gihe cyacu Cyo guseng' ucyeze.

ii.Yesu tugisenga, Suk' Umwuka Wera Mu mitima yacu, Atubwir' ibyawe.

iii.Kwinjira kwe kwera Kutumar' ubwoba N'agahinda kose, Tube mu mahoro.

iv.Tutabuzwa n'ibyo, Yes’ inzira yawe, Tuzayikomeza. Dufit' intege nshya.

v.Kandi tukigenda,Tuzategereza igihe cyo kuza Kwawe turi maso.
