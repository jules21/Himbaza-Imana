245. Iyo nibwiye mu mutima Igit’ Umwami yatangiyeho,Nzinukw' ibyo niratanaga; Mbireba nk'ibitariho rwose. 

ii.Sinkirat’ikindi, Mukiza, Keretse ko wankunz’ ukamfira;Ndeke n'ibyo nishimiraga, Ngushimir' uko wanyitangiye. 

iii.Ishavu n'ibambe bivanze Byavuye mu mutima wa Yesu;Mbese har’ undi Mwami  nka we Wateg’ ikamba nk'iryo ry'amahwa ? 

iv.Mwam’ Iyo mba mfit’ isi yose, Nkemera kuyitanga h' ubwishyu,Nta bwo yarangiza na hato Kwishyur' umwenda wanjy’ ukomeye. 

v.Urwo rukundo wankunz' utyo Rukwiriye kuntangisha byose,Ibyanjye n'umutima wanjye, Bikab' ibyaw’ ukabitegeka.
