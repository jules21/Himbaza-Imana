209.Yemw’ abari kuri Yesu, Dukanguke, tube maso, Kuko wa Mwanzi, Satani,: Ajy’ atureba ku jisho. 

ii.Satani ntiyigendera, Afit’ icy’ ahagazeho; Nuko ube maso, wirinde, Witwaz’ inkota y’Umwuka. 

iii.Ko uhishira Satani se, Kandi witirirwa Yesu ? None witind’ umwiyake : Afit’ igihe kigufi. 

iv.Yaciriw’ iteka ribi, Ashak’ abo yisasira. Bitum' ashishikarira Koshy’ abakunzi ba Yesu. 

v.Ibya Yesu twabikijwe Twihute tubimenyeshe , ab'isi yose,bihane, bave mu byaha,bakizwe.
