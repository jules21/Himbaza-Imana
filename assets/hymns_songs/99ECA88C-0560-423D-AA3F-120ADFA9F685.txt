15. Nimushim' izina ryiza ry'Uwiteka Rurema; N'icyubahiro cye cyinshi nimucyamamaze hose hose
Nimuvug' urukundo rwe, bose barwumve,Abo ku mpera y'isi bamenye kw akiza.

ref:urukundo rw'Uwiteka ruratemba nk'uruzi kand'ubuntu bwe n'ibambe ntabwo bizashir'iteka ryose

ii.Nimushim' Umwami Yesu : ni we Mwana w'Intama Wapfiriy’ abanyabyaha, hanyum' agahambwa
 mu gituro  Maze, kuwa gatatu, yaneshej' urupfu, Arazuk' arazamuka, yima mw ijuru.

iii.Kand' umuns' uzaza vuba, Yes' agaruke mw isi Kwim' ingoma ye n'ubwami mu cyubahiro n'ubwiza
 bwinshi, Isi n'ijuru byombi binezerwe hamwe N'inyamaswa n'inyoni n'ibyaremwe byose.

iv.Icyo gihe cy'ibirori, tuzaririmba twishimye, Twiteguye nk'umugeni, uko arimba
yitegur' umukwe; Kukw Umwam' azamanuka ngw az' adusange, Tub' imbata ze zera zinezerw' iteka.
