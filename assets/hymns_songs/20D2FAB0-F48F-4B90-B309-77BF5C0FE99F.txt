247.unyigishe Mukiza,Iby’ Umusaraba   N'icyatumy’ uza mw isi,Ukatubambirwa. Umenyeshe umumaro W'umunyabyah’ umwe, Umbwire n'igiciro Wanshungurishije !

ii.Unyigishe n'impamvu  Y'isok’ itukura Yo mu rubavu rwawe,Urwo bacumise, Ari jye gusa mw isi: Wacumuy’ Imana,  Uba warayavuye Ngw’amber’ impongano.

iii.unyerek'urukundo rutarondoreka rwamanutse i kuzimu ku bwacu abihebe uyu mutim'ukonje wuzuy'inarijye ,uwatsemwo urukundo ruva i Gologota

iv.unyigishe,Mukiza:ndi umunyabyaha pe icyantabara,mwami ni ubuntu bwonyine Yesu ni jy'ugukennye: mu bo wapfiriye b'abanyabyaha babi,ni jyewe ukabije

v.Mukiza wanjye,Yesu ni wowe nizeye maze ubwo umpamagaye,ndakwiyeguriye none ubwo unyakiriye,njye
 nshima imbabazi na rwa rukundo unkunda,mbivuge ndinde mfa.
