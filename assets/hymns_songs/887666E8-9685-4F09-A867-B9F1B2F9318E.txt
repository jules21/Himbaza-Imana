75. Umv' iri jambo n'iryo kwizerwa N'iryo kwemerwa n'abantu bose. Yuko twahaw' Umukiza Yesu. Yaje gukiza abanyabyaha. 

ii.Nar' uwa mbere mu banyabyaha, Ariko nsigaye nezerewe. Umukiza wanjye, Yesu Kristo, Yanyogesheje ya maraso ye. 

iii.Yesu Mukiz' ari hamwe natwe, Akor' imirimo nk'ukw ashaka. A can’ umuriro we muri twe. Ni nde wabasha kumuzitira? 

iv.N'ibipfamatwi bibasha kumva, Ndetse n'ibimuga bikagenda N'abanyabibembe barakizwa. Ubutumwa bwe buramamazwa. 

v.Mwami Yesu, duh' Umwuka Wera Umuduhuhireh' uyu munsi. Shayur' abarohamye mu byaha, Ubamurikire bagaruke. 

vi.Ugurur' ijur' ugush' imvura, Ahu magaye nko mu butayu Uhahindure kub' ahatoshye Havuz' impundu z'umunezero. 

vii.N'abapagani bakunamire Bagupfukamire bakuramye. Ukwiy' ishimwe ku bantu bose N'icyubahiro, Haleluya!
