102. Ump' akanya, Yesu, nze nkwegere, Kuko naniriwe mur' iyi si. Ndetse no mu gihe cy'intambara, Na bwo Mwami, ujy' ump' amahoro. 

ref:Ump' akanya, Yesu, nze nkwegere, Kuk' umwijim'ukabije mw isi. Icyo nkeneye n'ukukwegera Kugira ngo numv' ijambo ryawe. 

ii.Ump' akanya, Yesu, nze nkwegere Mu bingerageza n'amakuba. Ni wowe buhungiro nizeye Mu gihe cy'akaga mur' iyi si. 

iii.Ump' akanya, Yesu, nze nkwegere Kuko wanyikorerey' ibyaha. Ndumva nshaka kukubwira byose Kuko, Mwami, nizeye k'unyumva.
