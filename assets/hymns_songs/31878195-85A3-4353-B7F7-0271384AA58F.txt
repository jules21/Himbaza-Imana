iii.icyo tugushakaho Kirut' ibindi byose N'uko watuboneza, Twirirwe dutunganye, Twirind' iby' udashima, Yesu, tugushimishe.

iv.Tuz' intege nke zacu igice gusa, Mwami. Nyamara. Mucunguzi, Wowe ntutuyoberwa : Uz' akaga turimo, Nuk' ujy'uturengera.
