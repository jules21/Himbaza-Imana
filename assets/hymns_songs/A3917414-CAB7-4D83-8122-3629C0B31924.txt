200.Umwam'agaby'ingabo ze Kurwana n'ibyaha.Umugaba n' Umwana we : intwari ye ni nde? 

ii.Wikorer' umusaraba,Ugendane na We. Mufatany' imibabaro, Nuk' ub' intwari ye ! 

iii.Sitefano yakubiswe Kubw' uwo Mwami we, Yasabirag' abanzi be, Ubwo bamwicaga. 

iv.Ubwo yar' agihagaze,Yabay' intwari ye; Bamwicishij' amabuye, Areba mw ijuru. 

v.Batwikwaga mu muriro, Baranabambwaga. Dukurikiz' urugero Rwiza baduhaye ! 

vi.Bajugunyirwag' intare, Zikabatanyura. Babicishaga n'inkota : N' intwari za Yesu. 

vii.Non' izo ntwari za Yesu Zageze mw ijuru ! Dore zigos' intebe ye; Ziramusingiza ! 

viii.Zaciye mu mibabaro, Zizamukirayo. Mwam'udufashe natwe ngo Duc' aho baciye.
