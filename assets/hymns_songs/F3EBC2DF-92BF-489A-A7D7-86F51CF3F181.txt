207. Uwab’atinyutse ibyago byose Naz’akurikire—Umwami Yesu ! Nta cyamurekesha—iyo migambi ye Yo guca mu nzira —ijya mw’ ijuru !

ii.Ntabw’ azacogozwa N 'inshamugongo. Abazimubwira—Nibo bazagwa ! Abanzi be bose—Nti bamugarura munzir’ ahisemo— Ijya mw ijuru.

iii.Mwam’ ujy'undindisha—Umwuka wawe, mbone kuzaragwa Kubahw iteka. Have wa bwoba we—Nimunyihorere! Namaramaje pe— Kujya mw’ ijuru.
