 254.Nimureb’igitangaza: wa munyamubabaro yanesheje wa mugome;tumukomer’ amashyi:Haleluya,Haleluya Tumwimike, n ‘umwami

ii.Tumuh' ikamba ry'ubwiza,Ngo tumushengerere. Tumuh' Intebe y'ubwami.Twese tuvuz' Impundu :Haleluya, Haleluya  ni we Nyir'imbaraga 

iii. Mwambur' Ikamba ry'amahwa Bamwambitse baseka. Mwambik' Ikamba ry'Ubwami,Mwemere kw ar' Umwami.Haleluya, Haleluya  Tegeka,Nyir'ingabo 

iv.Vuz’ Impundu, rangurura : Yanesheje Satani. Ari mu twicarabami, Tumukurir' ubwatsi. Haleluya,Haleluya ni We Mwami w'abami.
