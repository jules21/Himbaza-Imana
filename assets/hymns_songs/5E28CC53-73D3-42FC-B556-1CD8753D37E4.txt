401.Iri  joro, yemwe bakristo  mwese,Turabibuts' Umwana w'Imana, Uko yaje kuducungura twese,Ngw Iman' izadukure mu byaha.
 Abo mw isi bose banezerewe Iri joro, bibuk' Umukiza. Mupfukame, mushim’Umucunguzi ! Tunezerwe, tunezerwe twese ! (x2)
                                                                                                                                                        
ii.Kwizigira kwacu, bakristo mwese,Kudutere guhimbaz' Umwami; Inyenyeri y’ iracyaka kw ijuru Ahw abanyabwenge bayibonye. Uwo Mwami
 yashyizwe mu muvure: Mubimenye, rubanda mwese mwe ! Munezerwe kukw Iman' ibikiza. Munezerwe, mwambaz' uwo Mwami ! (x 2)

iii. Umukiza yabakuye mu kaga No mu buretwa bw'umwanzi mubi. Mwes' abita ben' Imana Rurema; Abigish' iby'urukundo rwayo.
 Tumwerurire yuko tumukunda:Yadupfiriye ku Musaraba. Rubanda mwe, mwishime cyane none ! Tunezerwe, tunezerwe twese ! (x 2)
