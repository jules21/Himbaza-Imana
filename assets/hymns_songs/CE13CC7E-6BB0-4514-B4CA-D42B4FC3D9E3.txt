27.Mutabazi wacu Yesu, Twese tuguhimbaze; Ni wowe wadupfiriye,Yesu Mwana w'Intama.

ref:Yes' ashimwe. Yes' ashimwe Icyubahiro kibe Icy' uwo Mwana w' Intama. Duhimbaze izina rye.

ii.Twar’imbohe za Satani,Twari mu mubabaro; Yatuboheshej’ ingoyi, Yes' aratubohora.

iii.Tuguh' imibiri yacu N'imitima n'ubwenge; Kand’ibyo dufite byose Bib' ibyawe, Mukiza.

iv.Tukwijejwe cyanecyane N’ uk' ushobora rwose Kuturinda no kutwoza Ibyo twagucumuye.
