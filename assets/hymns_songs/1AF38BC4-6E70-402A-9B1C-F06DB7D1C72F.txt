383.Icyamp' ukanyikorerera Amaganya yanjye yose, Yesu, Ngaturiz' Uwiteka, Nkamenya yukw ibyanjye byose Ubitegekesh'ubugwaneza N'ubwenge burutaho.

ii. Kenshi ntiny' ibiribubeho, Ngahagarik' umutima, Mwami, Ngize kwizera guke.Icyampa nkarorera rwose Kwishingikiriza  ku by'iyi si, Nkakwizera wenyine.

iii.Nabuzwa n'iki kuyisaba, Nkajya nyisunga, nkayikoreza Ayo maganya yose,Ngahumurizwa no kumenya Yuko Dat' ugaburir' inyoni Azanyumvira nanjye ?

iv.Ntitwizer' uko bikwiriye; Umutim' uhagaz' ujugunya Rwos' amahoro yawo. Arikw inyoni n'uburabyo Binyigisha  k' umuns' ukwiriwe N'ibyawo byago gusa.

v.Umpe kumeny' ibyo nigishwa N'uburabyo n'inyoni, Mukiza, Nkwishingikirizeho ! Ngez' ah' ushaka, Mushorera,  Nuko menye, nkiri mu makuba, Amahor' adashira.
