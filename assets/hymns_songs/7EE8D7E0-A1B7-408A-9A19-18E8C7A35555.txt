60.We, mutim' urira,—Uze kwa Yesu; Amakuba yawe,—Uyamubwire.  Mubwire buhoro—Ibikuriza; We kugir' ubwoba,—Kandi wirira.

ii.Bwira Yesu byose,—N' inshuti yawe, Ibikubabaza—N'ibigutera.  Arakund' afasha—Abashavuye; Wizer' ijambo rye,—Kandi wirira.

iii.Abaremerewe—N'ibiruhanya, Babwire kw Imana—Yabaruhura.  Ubahumurize,—Ut' Umukiza Amar' agahinda :—None, mwirira !
