158.Umvir' ijwi rya Yesu, Ni we Mukiza wawe. Umv’arakubaz’ ati: Munyabyah' urankunda ?
 
ii.Umugore yabasha  Kwibagirw' umwana we?  Nahw ibyo byashoboka,  Jyeho nzajya nkwibuka.
 
iii.Ni jye wakubohoye, Nkuvur’ibikomere;Ni jye waguhabuye;  Ni jye mucy'ukwakira. 

iv.Kand’urukundo rwanjye Ntirubasha gushira; Rurut' urundi rwose, Kuko rutarangira. 

v.Mu gihe gikwiriye, Nzakujyana mw ijuru; Uzabon' ibyishimo : Mbes' urankunda cyane ? 

vi.Mwami, birambabaje K' urukundo ngukunda ari ruke hanyuma Y'urwo rukundo rwawe. 

vii.Ndagukunda buhoro, Nubwo bidahebuje. Nuk' umpe kurushaho Kugukunda, Mukiza.
