151.Yes’ arush’ abandi bose—Kudukunda; Arush' ukw abavandimwe—Bakundana. Nahw izindi nshuti zacu Zaduhararuka zose, Yesu ntabw’ ahemukira—Ab’ akunda.

ii.N'iki gihesh’ ubugingo—Budashira ? S’ ukumenya Yesu wacu—Udukunda ? Twarazimiye nk'intama. Yes' aradushakashaka, Atubony’ atwinjiz' ubwo—Mu rugo rwe

iii.Tumumenye kw ar' inshuti—Yishimira Kuduh' imigisha yose—N'imbabazi. Imitima yac’ ikunda Kumvir’ iyi Nshuti yacu No kumwizigira cyane—Tudatinya.

iv.Kand’ ibyaha byacu byari—Bikabije: Yes’ aterwa n'urukundo—Kudupfira : Ni byo bituneshereza; Ni byo biturind' ibibi, Tukayoboka yanzira—Iger’ i We.
