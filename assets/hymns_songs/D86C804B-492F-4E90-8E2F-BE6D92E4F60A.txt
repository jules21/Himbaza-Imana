66.Nageze ku Mwami Yesu None mu mutima wanjye Harimw izuba ry'ubuntu Rimurik' iminsi yose. 

ref:Umutim' uraririmba Yuko nabohowe rwose. Negerey' Umwami Yesu Ndirimban' umunezero. 

ii.Umuriri w'ubuntu bwe Uri mu mutima wanjye. Agakiza ke muri jye Ni nk'umuraba mu mazi. 

iii.Non' Umwami Yesu Kristo Na muberey' ubuturo! Ndetse nahaw' Umufasha Umwuka w'isezerano! 

iv.Ubw' Umwuk' azabazaho Muzaherako mumenya, Yuko nicaranye namwe Byavuzwe n'Umwami Yesu. 

v.N'ukuri yatugezemo Asigay' abana natwe Ngwino wih' Imana Yera Yoz' uwo mutima wawe. 

vi.Ubwo Yes' azagaruka, Aje mu bwiza bw'ijuru. Nzahindurwa nse na Yesu Ndusheho gushim' Imana!
