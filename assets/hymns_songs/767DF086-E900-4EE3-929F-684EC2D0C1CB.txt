174.Jy'uba maso, wowe muntu wizeye :Mu gitondo, jya wisung’ Imana, Kuk' Umwanzi yoshy' abataba maso Ngw abagushe kukw abanga. 

ref:Jy'uba maso : wicogora  Jy'uba maso : senga  

ii.Jy'uba maso, no kumanyva y'ihangu, Iby'isi byibagij' iby'ijuru; Weho, gir' umwanya wo kwiherera, Ng’ useng' Umukiza wawe. 

iii.Jy'uba maso n'imunsi na n'ijoro, Wibuk' ibyiza by'inshuti Yesu; Ujy'umushakish' umutima wawe : Mubwir' ibikubabaje. 

iv.Jy'uba maso, hose mu gihe cyose : Satan' akubikiriye hose, Ashak' uburyo yaguseseramo, Ngo yimur' Umwami Yesu
