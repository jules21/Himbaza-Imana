175.Mwunger’ udukunda,—Jy' uturagira, N'uduhora hafi,—Twatiny' iki se? Tugukurikire, Iy' ujya hose, Naho haba habi—Cyangwa se heza.

ii.Mwunger' udukunda,—Tuz' ijwi ryawe, Uduhamagaye,—Turanezerwa Kand' iy’ uduhannye, Turabyemera. Tur' intama zawe :—Utwiharire.

iii.Wavuy' amaraso,—Mwungeri mwiza, Kugira ngw intama—Uducungure. Washyize kuri twe Ikimenyetso,Ni cyo Mwuka Wera,—Udutuyemo.

iv.Mwunger' uturinde,—Uturengere Amasega mabi—Ye  kutwegera. Nta cy' azadutwara, Kuk' ukomeye. Turikumwe, byose—Biranesheka.
