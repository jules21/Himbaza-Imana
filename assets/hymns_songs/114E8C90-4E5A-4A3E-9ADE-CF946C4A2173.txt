10.Yesu wonger' unyigishe iby' umusaraba. Ni wo soko nziza cyane, niyo yoz' ibyaha.

ref:Umusaraba wa Yesu, Niwo nsingiz' ubu. Yes' undindire muri wo Mbone kukumenya.

ii.Niho naboney' ubuntu bwawe butangaje. No mur' uwo musaraba, havuyemw umucyo.

iii.Ndindira muri wo, Yesu kand' unamenyeshe. Uko wanyikorerey' ibyaha byanjye byose.

iv.Munsi y'uwo musaraba handindir' iteka. Ngukunde kuv' uyu munsi ngez' iteka ryose.
