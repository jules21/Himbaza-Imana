9.Yes’ashimwe turamushim’umukiza Hose bahimbaz’urukundo rwe Mumushime ngabo zo mw’ijuru mwese Muhimbaz’ubuntu n’ubwiza bwe Ukw’intama zirindwa n’umushumba Niko Yes’arind’abana be Mumushime ngabo mw ijuru mwese Mutanarir’urwo rukundo rwe.

ii.Yes’ashimwe turamushim’umukiza kubw’ibyaha byacu yapfiriye yaje mw’isi ngw’atubohore adukize aduh’ubugingo budashira yarabambw’abyemejwe n’urukundo abagome baramuhemukira yambikwa n’ikamba ry’amahwa mu mutwe byos’abyemezwa n’urukundo rwe

iii.Yes’ashimwe turamushim’umukiza yarazutse yicara mw’ijuru non’ashimwe n’abari mw’ijuru rirerire none twese ntitugitiny’urupfu nyagasani wararunesheje ahubwo rwahinduw’irembo ry’ijuru no kuzaka kwawe  mucunguzi
