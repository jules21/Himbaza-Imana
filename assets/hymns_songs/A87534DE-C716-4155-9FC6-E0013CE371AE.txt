335.Umuhanuzi Yesaya Yeretsw' ibyiza kera. Yerekw' Imana mw ijuru Iri mu rusengero : Abakerubi n'Abaserafi Bayishyize hagati, Bajya bayishim' iteka, Bayisingiza bati:

ii.Mwam' Uwer' Uwer' Uwera:Ibyo mw isi n'ijuru Byuzuyemw ishimwe ryawe, Bose baguhimbaze. Baracyaririmba batyo, Kand' abantu bo mw isi Na bo barashim' Imana, Umukiza n'Umwami.

iii.Natwe tuyihimbazanye N' abamarayika bose, Tuti : Mwami nyir'ingabo, Ni Wow' usumba byose; Ur' Uwer' Uwer' Uwera : Ibyo mw isi n'ijuru Byuzuyemw ishimwe ryawe. Bose baguhimbaze !
