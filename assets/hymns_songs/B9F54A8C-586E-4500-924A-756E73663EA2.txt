359.Iyo ntinye ko kwizera Kwanjy' ari guke, Kand’ iyo ngiye kuneshwa, Yesu ni W' umfata. 

Ref:Yes' aramfata, Akankomeza, Kukw ankunda bihebuje : Arankomeza. 

ii.Urukundo rwanjye ruke Ntirwamufata. Sinamugundir' ubwanjye : Yesu ni W' umfata. 

iii.Ntiyandeka, kukw ankunda.Nubwo ndi mubi; Anyishimir' ubwo nd' uwe ; itek' aramfata. 

iv.Ntiyakundir' ubugingo Bwanjye kubura; Yabucunguj' igiciro : Itek' aramfata
