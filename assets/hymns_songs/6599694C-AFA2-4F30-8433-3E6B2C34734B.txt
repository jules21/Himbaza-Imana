403.Nyenyeri yo mu museke, Ubonets' uri kw ijuru,Ur' umucy' utuvira. Tumurikir' imitima, Utweyurir' umwijima Ukunda kuba mw isi.  

ref:Mwami, Mwana—w'Uwiteka, Muri Wowe,—har’ umucyo: Jy udushorera, Mukiza. 

ii.Ni Wowe, Yesu, wanshatse Mu mwijima wa Satani,Ubwo nari muri wo, Wowe, Mucunguzi wanjye, Wankuye mu bwenge buke Kubw' urukundo rwawe. 

iii.Wiyambuy' icyubahiro, Wigira nk'umugaragu Utagir' agaciro. Uhorw' ibyaha nakoze, Mpeshw' amahoro n'ubuntu N'urupfu rwawe, Yesu. 

iv.Mucunguzi, mbane nawe, Njye nkwizeran’umurava :Uz' intege nke zanjye; Njye nkor’ iby' ushaka byose. Non' unshunshumurireho Umwuka wawe Wera.
