55.Yes' araduhamagara kureka ibyaha byose :ahora atubwira iteka ngo tumukurikire.

ii.Tumere nka ba barobyi, Bumvis' amagambo ye; Bemeye gusig' ibyabo, Ngo bamukurikire.

iii.Atubuza kwifuz' ibyo Mw isi n'icyubahiro; Azaduh' ibiduhaza, Ni tumukurikira.

iv.Nubwo twaba tunezerwa, Cyangwa turi mu byago, Yes' araduhamagara, Ngo tumukurikire.

v.Yes' araduhamagara;Nuko, tub' intore ze, Tumukunde, tumusenge, Tuzageze ku gupfa.
