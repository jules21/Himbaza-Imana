95. Ibyaha byanjye, Yesu Yarabyikoreye, W' atigeze gukora Icyaha na kimwe;  Ndamwegera, ndasenga, Nd' umunyabyaha pe, Nizer' ijambo ry' ati : Urababariwe !

ii.Maz' ibindushya byose Ndabimukorera. Nta wund' utari Yesu Wabyihanganira.Ndamwegera, njy' ahw ari, Ndushye, ntentebutse, Ndagenda, mpaw' intege, Nsindishirijwe pe !

iii.Nukw amaganya yanjye,Nyakurwaho na We, Nduhukira muri We, Amp' amahoro ye.  Imanuweli Kristo, Zina rihebuje ! Rimbera nk' imibavu, Ihumura neza.

iv.Icyampa nkaba nka We,Akamp' ineza ye, N'imbabazi n'ubuntu N'ubugwaneza bye.  Yes' uzampe gutura I wawe mw ijuru, Nkajya ngusingizanya N'abamarayika.
