50. Ririmb' inkuru nziza: Iman' i kunda bose. Wigishe ya maraso yatwejej' imitima. Uvug' iby' iyo mpano, yuko twahaw' Umwana.  Gend' uvug' iyo nkuru ku bantu bose. 

Ref: Yesu ku musaraba yadupfiriye twese. Yatwujuje n'Imana, ni yo Data wa twese. Wa mwenda war' ahera, nziko watabutsemo.
 Jye nawe, none twugururiw' inzira.

ii. Abababaye bose, gend' ubaririmbire. N'abari mu ntambara cyan' ibagerageza. Ririmba mu misozi, vug' iyi nkuru nziza,  Yuko Yes' abashaka kubw' urukundo.

iii. Mu mwijim' uririmbe, naho hatav' izuba. Bos' ubaririmbire, bos' uko bamerewe, Mu gitond' uririmbe, no ku manywa y'ihangu.  Shim' Umukiza nubw' ijoro riguye.
