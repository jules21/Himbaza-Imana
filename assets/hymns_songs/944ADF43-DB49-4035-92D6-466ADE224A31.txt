225.Mw ivuka ryawe, Yesu, bakuryamishije Mu kiraro kigawa, mu muvure w'inka. Wasasiwe, Mukiza, ahadakwiriye. Nubw' ur' Uwaturemye, ni ko wagiriwe!

ii.Nimuze, yemwe bana, tumuhimbarize Ko yaryamishijw' atyo, mu muvure w'inka Dushim' Umwami Yesu, tumukundir' ibyo Ko yabay' umukene kubw' abanyabyaha

iii.Umfashe, Mwami Yesu, kuguhora hafi. Ngo ngukunde, tuban' iminsi yanjye yose, Kandi n'abandi bana,  nabo ubiyereke Bagukunde, dushiman' urukundo rwawe.
