5.Duhimbaz ’Uwitek’ Imana no kuyisenga. Kukw ari yo yaremy’ ibyo mu isi n’ iby’ ijuru Yesu  mwami wez’ imitima yacu Ngo turirimbir’Imana.

ii.Duhimbaz’ Uwitek’ Imana no kuyisenga  kukw’ ijy’ itegeka neza  mu bihugu izaduh’ ibidukwiye byose, byose twizer ’iri jambo ryayo.

iii.Duhimbaz’ uwitek’ Imana no kuyisenga yaraturemye; ni yo yaduhay’ ubugingo; iturinda mu byago byacu byose; ni y’ itugirir’ ubuntu.

iv.Duhimbaz’ uwitek’ Imana no kuyisenga.Ibihumeka byose bishim’ izina ryayo;Tuyikunde kukw ari umucyo wacu;Tuyisab’ iminsi yose.
