23.Mana yo mw ijuru, Byos' uri nyirabyo, Kand' ubiduh' ubudasiba. Utang’ utimana; Ujy' udukenura, Bituma tuguhimbaza. 

ii.Impan' ihebuje Yes’ atugabira N' ubuntu bwe n'amahoro ye. Yatubabariye, Twaramugomeye : Mbes’ ibyo s’ igitangaza? 

iii.Mwami, ndakwinginze, Nubwo ntakwiriye, Nyigiza hafi, tugumane ! Mpa kugukorera, Ndusheho kwemera Gukund’ ibiri mw ijuru !
