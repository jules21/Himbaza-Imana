381.Yes' avukiye mw isi, Yakuriye muri yo, Yigishwa n' Imana, Se, Gusobanukirw' isi. 

ii.Yesu yitegereje Ubugome bwo mw isi; Areby' uko bungana, Arababara rwose. 

iii.Nukw asang' ab'iyi si Banze gukiranuka; Bamuyoberwa rwose, Bati : Si we mukiza. 

iv.Yes' abony' ibyo byose Arabababarira,Ngo n' abo gucungurwa No kubon' igitambo. 

v.Nukw amenya kw ari We Uwiteka yatanze Kubaber' igitambo Cyo kubahongerera. 

vi.Ntiyashidikany' ubwo, Yemera guhemurwa, Yiteguzwa n'Imana Kudupfir' abo mw isi. 

vii.Maz' abantu bo mw isi Bamubamba ku giti, Bamubabaza cyane :Ni ko yadukijije. 

viii.Mwami Yes' ushimirwe Ubwo buntu wagize! Wemeye kumbambirwa : Nzahora ngusingiza !
