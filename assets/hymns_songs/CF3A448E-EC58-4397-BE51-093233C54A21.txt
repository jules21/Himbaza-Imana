222.Mbese tuzahurirayo, Kuri wa mugezi mwiza Uca mw ijuru hagati, Uv' i bwami ku Mana yacu ? 

ref:Koko tuzahurirayo, Tubonane n'abera ku mugezi, Ku mugez' utunganye w' Imana Uv' i bwami ku Mana yacu. 

ii.Tuzagendana ku nkombe Y'umugezi w'ubugingo. Tuzasengan' Uwiteka, Tuyisingiz' iteka ryose. 

iii.Nuko tutarahagera, Duturw' imitwaro yose, Dukizwe n'ubuntu bwayo : Izatwambik' imyenda yera. 

iv.Tuzahurira n'abera Ku mugez' ubonerana; Nta kizadutandukanya Kuk' urupfu rutagerayo. 

v.Nuko, tuzagera vuba Kur' uwo mugezi mwiza, Turirimbane n'abaho Indirimbo zishimishije.
