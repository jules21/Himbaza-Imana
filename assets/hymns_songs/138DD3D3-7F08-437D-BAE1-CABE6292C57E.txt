18.a. Umwungeri wacu ni we Yesu; Ajy' arinda intama ze. Aturagira mu bwatsi bwiza; Ni W' utubumbatira. 
Yes' Umwami, Yes' Umwami  Ni We waducunguye :X2

ii.Ni twe ntama zawe, Mwami Yesu ;Utuyubor' inzira ! Uturind' ibyago n'amakuba, Udukure mu byaha ! 
Yes' Umwami, Yes' Umwami,  Mutabazi, twumvire !X2

iii.Yesu yemeye kutwakir' ubu,Nubwo dukiranirwa. Amaraso y' aratwuhagira; Niwe munyambabazi ! 
Yes' Umwami, Yes' Umwami,  Twihute, tumwitabe.X2

iv.Yes' utwemere tw' abagusanze, Twihannye, tubabaye. Ur' Umwami kand' ur' Umukiza; Tur' abawe kuv' ubu !
 Yes' Umwami, Yes' Umwami. Dor' abakwiragije ! X2
