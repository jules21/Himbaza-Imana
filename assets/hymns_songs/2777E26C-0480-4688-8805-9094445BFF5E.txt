166.Ngwino, soko y'umugisha; Mp' umutim' ugushima, Ntabw' ukam' iteka ryose;Njye ngusingiza cyane. Nyigisha guhora nsenga; menye n'ubwiza bwawe, Nizigir' ubwami bwawe; Nyuzuz' urukund' ubu !

ii.Nyibutsa bya byiza byawe Byose wakankoreye. Nizeye yukw uzanjyana, Ukangeza mw ijuru. Yesu, waranshatse, mpabye; Nari kure yaw' ubwo, Unyigur' urupfu rubi, Wemera kumbambirwa.

iii.Ndi mu mwenda wawe, Mwami,W'ubuntu wangiriye; Unyigize hafi yawe, meny' imbabazi zawe.Satan' ajy'angerageza Ngo nindek' unkund' atyo. Akir' umutima wanjye, Nuk' undinde, mb' uwawe.
