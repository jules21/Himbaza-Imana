272.Umwam' ageze kw irembo, Wabambwe ku Musaraba; Yaje kudukorany' abe,Tubane mw ijuru. 

ref:Kw irembo  Kw irembo ! Ub' ari kw irembo! Nukw araje, nukw araje, Kand' ageze kw irembo. 

ii.Ibyerekana kuza kwe Birihuta kuboneka : Agiye kutugabira Kubaho kw'iteka. 

iii.Ntihazabur' intambara; Ntabw' amahor' azahora. Keretse Yes' atsembyeho Urupfu n'ibyaha. 

iv.Maze, mw isi yagizwe nshya, Tuzahaba, dusugire, Kudapfa kwakuye gupfa,Tutakibabara.
