70. Reka gutiny' ibizakubaho Iman' izakurinda! Reb' ubushake bw'Imana gusa Iman' irakurinda. 

ref:Iman' irakurinda Iminsi yose mu nzir' ucamo. Iraguhaz' amahoro. Iman' irakurinda.  

ii.Nubw' uwo mutwar' uremereye, Iman' irakurinda. N'ibyago biri mu nzir' ucamo, Iman' irakurinda. 

iii.Iman' imeny' ubukene bwawe, Iman' irakurinda. Komez' ubane n'Imana gusa, Iman’ irakurinda. 

iv.Kand' ubw' uzaca mu bikomeye, Iman' izakurinda. Uyimenyesh' ibikubabaje, Iman'irakurinda.
