208.Imana n’ ubuhingiro :Ni Yo ngab’ idukingira.iyo turi mu makuba, Ni Y’ ibasha kudukiza. Uk’ Umwanzi wacu Yishakiy’ uburyo Bwo kudutsinda, Ntatubasha na gato, Kukw’ Iman’ iturengera.

ii.Ni twirwanaho twenyine,tubura imbaraga rwose.ariko dufit'intwari ijy'iturwanira iteka.iyo ntwari ni nde? ni wowe Mukiza.Mugaba wacu,tuzi yuko mu byago ari wowe uturengera.

iii.Iy' abagome bahuje Guter' itorero ryawe, Urarirengera, Mana, Ntibabashe kuritsinda. Nubw’ Umugambanyi;Ashaka kutwica, Ntadushyikira Kukw’ aneshwa n’ijambo Ry'Imana, Nyir'imbaraga. 

iv. Hishur’ijambo rinesha, Ridufasha dushobewe. Imbabazi zawe nyinshi Zize, zituzeho twese. Twakwamburw' ibyacu Ndetse, tukabohwa, Ntitwabyitaho : Byose tuzabinesha; Tuzaragwa, n’ubwami bwawe !
