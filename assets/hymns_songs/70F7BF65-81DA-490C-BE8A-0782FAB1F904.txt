154.Ndabaririmbira—Iby’Uwamfiriye, Wankunze mfit' ibyaha, narararutse Wambonyemw iki, Krisito,  Ukaza mw is’ukamfira ?

ii. Yasiz' ingoma ye,—Nukw aramanuka; Baramusuzugura, ntibamwemera.Har’ indi nshuti nka We se  Yakund' abayihinyuye ?

iii. Baramushingije,—Bashash'imyambaro Ngo, Hozana; hahirw' Umwami Krisito!Bukeye, barahinduka, Bati: Nabambwe ! Nabambwe !

iv. Bafash' Umwam’ubwo,—Baramuhemura; Bakiz’umwambuzi, babamba Krisito. Iby' arabyihanganira,  Ngw abon' ukw aducungura.

v.  Yavuye mw ijuru,—Aza mur' iyi si, Abur' aho yarambik' umusaya we; Amfira ku Musaraba,  Ngo nanjy' angeze mw ijuru.

vi. Ntabwo nzarambirwa—Kubaririmbira Iby' urukundo rwe n'uko yababajwe. Yanyemeye nk'inshuti ye:  Nzavuga hos' izina rye!
